# Notes on scanning the state of the converted lessons

## What needs checking and changing / adding

1. Some lessons have extractions from worksheets and/or slides that show concepts really well but miss som eof the core concepts. For example the lesson on network topologies has an extract showing star and bus network topologies but misses out the part from the origional material that shows ring and mesh networks. The extracts of material that explain ring and mesh network topologies should also be extracted and added to the lesson. There are many examples of this throughout the lessons.
2. Some lessons have tasks expected to be completed by the pupils but the tasks are not stated on the worksheets and sometimes there is nowhere for the pupils to upload their work or add a screenshot of their work. Uploading the work is prefered.
3. Sometimes documents are shown or provided in the lesson. They should be viewable by the teacher and pupils from the system if they are required by the lesson or useful for the lesson. 
4. The slides should state when the next activity is to be started by the pupils and what they are to do in the slide that is presented as the pupils should start their lesson. The idea being that the slide prompts the teacher to get the pupils to start the part of the worksheet or activitty that they should be starting.
5. Some of the programming lessons are missing code that is used in the lessons and needed for the worksheets.
6. Before starting this search through the lessons please assess what else might be missing from the lessons that might be needed or useful for the teacher and pupils.
