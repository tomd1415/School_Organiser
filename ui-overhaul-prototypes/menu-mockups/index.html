ADHD/ASD Rationale: Option 2 (Calm HUD)

  • Zero Persistent Clutter: The menu vanishes entirely when you are working. Peripheral vision is protected from layout noise, preventing focus shifts.
  • Spatial Predictability: Tapping the hover bar opens a 3x3 layout of links. Autistic teachers benefit from items always sitting at the exact same coordinates.
  • Zero-Friction Hotkeys: Instantly jump anywhere on the LAN application in two keystrokes (e.g., double space -> `g` + `a` for marking).

Scaffolded Ribbon Simulator

1. Hover your cursor over the **left vertical ribbon** to expand the labels from icon-only mode.

2. Click the **⚙️ Advanced Drawer** tab at the bottom to expand/collapse setup settings.

ADHD/ASD Rationale: Option 3 (3-Tier Ribbon)

  • Cognitive Urgency Split: Links are scaffolded. High-importance/safeguarding is at the top, daily checklists in the middle, and long-term settings are gated at the bottom.
  • Icon-Only by Default: Collapses to a 60px strip, preserving width for multi-screen dashboard displays, and expands smoothly on hover.
  • Permanent Visual Anchor: Always exists in the same vertical margin, supporting spatial reassurance without shifting the center workspace text layout.
