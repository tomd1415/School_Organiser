## What we are learning
Data scientists follow the same five steps every time. This is the **investigative cycle**, called **PPDAC**.

## Put the cycle in order
Drag the five steps of the investigative cycle into the right order.

```order
Problem — decide the question you want to answer
Plan — decide what data you need and how to get it
Data — collect or find the data
Analysis — make charts and look for patterns
Conclusion — answer the question and report what you found
```
