## What we are learning
Today I will change the size, colour, and style of text, and edit a party invitation.

## Sort the change
Drag each change into the right box.

```sort
Font size: make the title bigger, make small words larger
Font colour: turn the text red, change the words to blue
Font style: pick a fancy or bubbly font, choose a new look for the letters
```
