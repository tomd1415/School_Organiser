# Becoming a designer

## Today we are learning
- choose the best place for my content
- paste text and add pictures to my cover
- change content after I have added it

> 🧑‍🏫 Read the three "I can…" aloud. Same routine. Vocabulary on the board: desktop publishing · copy · paste. Have last week's templates ready to open.

## Find last week's work
- open the desktop publishing software
- find your saved magazine template

> 🧑‍🏫 Remind them where saved work lives (in Canva, "recent designs"). TA: prompt the path, do not click it for them. Likely error: starting a new blank page — fix-words "open the one you already made."

## Add your text
- click a placeholder box
- type your text, or copy and paste it in

> 🧑‍🏫 I-do: model typing into a box, then copying a line and pasting it. Some pupils can use the sample magazine text if they have none planned. This is the "order" task on the worksheet.

## Add a picture
- search for a picture
- drag it onto your cover

> 🧑‍🏫 Follow your school's rules for pupils searching for images. Keep choices simple and safe.

## A finished cover
![A finished magazine cover with a title, picture, story and quote]({{res:l4-finished-magazine-cover-example-teach-computing.png}})

- title, picture, story and a quote
- everything in its place

> 🧑‍🏫 We-do: compare to this example. Ask where the title and picture sit. Sets up the Challenge: what if a picture covers the title? (move it, or make it smaller).

## Make changes
- move things that overlap
- make a picture bigger or smaller
- delete anything you do not want

> 🧑‍🏫 Designers change things until they look right. TA: prompt, do not redesign it for them.

## I can…
- tick your three "I can…"
- show me one thing you added and one thing you changed

> 🧑‍🏫 Note who can copy, paste and move on their own. Save the cover.
