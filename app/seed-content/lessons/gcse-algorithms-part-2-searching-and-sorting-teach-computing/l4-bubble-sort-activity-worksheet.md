## One pass of a bubble sort
A bubble sort compares two items that are **next to each other** and swaps them if they are in the wrong order. It does this all the way along the list — that is called one **pass**.

![One pass of a bubble sort on a row of cards: each step highlights the two cards being compared, and the largest card ends up at the right]({{res:l4-one-pass-of-a-bubble-sort-teach-computing.png}})

## Put the steps in order
Drag the steps for ONE pass of a bubble sort into order.

```order
Start at the first item in the list
Compare the item to the one next to it
If it is bigger, swap the two items
Move to the next item
Repeat to the end of the list
```
