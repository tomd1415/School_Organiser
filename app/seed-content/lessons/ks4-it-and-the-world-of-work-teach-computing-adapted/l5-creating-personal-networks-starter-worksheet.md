## What is an ad hoc network?
![A phone connected to a laptop by a cable — tethering]({{res:l5-tethering-a-phone-to-a-laptop-teach-computing.png}})

An AD HOC network is a quick, temporary, device-to-device connection. A PAN is a Personal Area Network — a small network around one person.
