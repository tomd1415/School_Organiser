## What we are learning

## Starter — what is the cloud?
"The cloud" means software and storage you use over the internet, on many different devices.

## Functions or features?
A FUNCTION is a service the cloud provides. A FEATURE is a quality that makes it good. Drag each card into the right group.

```sort
Function (a service it provides): Software as a service (SaaS), Infrastructure as a service (IaaS), Platform as a service (PaaS)
Feature (a good quality): Easy access, Cost-effectiveness, Security, Scalability
```
