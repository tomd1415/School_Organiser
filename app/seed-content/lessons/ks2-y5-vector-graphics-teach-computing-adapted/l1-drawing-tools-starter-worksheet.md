## What we are learning
Today I will learn that vector drawings are made from shapes and lines, and I will find the drawing tools.

## Vector drawings are everywhere
A **vector drawing** is a picture made on a computer from **shapes** and **lines**. Logos, signs and app icons are vector drawings.
