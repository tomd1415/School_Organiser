## 🟡 Core — vector or paint?
A **vector** drawing is made from shapes you can move and resize. A **paint** drawing is made from coloured **pixels** (dots) that you cannot move on their own.

```sort
Vector drawing: made from shapes
Vector drawing: shapes can be moved and resized
Paint drawing: made from coloured pixels (dots)
Paint drawing: you colour in like a brush
```
