## What we are learning

## The Arrange menu
Right-click a selection to **group** or **ungroup** it.

![The menu showing Ungroup]({{res:l5-ungroup-menu.png}})

## Group some objects
Put the steps to group objects in order.

```order
Drag a box around the objects you want
Right-click one of the selected objects
Choose Group
Now drag the whole group together
```

## 🟢 Support
Sort each job into group or ungroup.

```sort
Group: move lots of objects together
Group: copy a whole picture at once
Ungroup: change the colour of just one part
Ungroup: move only one shape
```
