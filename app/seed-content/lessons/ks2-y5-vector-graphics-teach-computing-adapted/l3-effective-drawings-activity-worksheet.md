## What we are learning
Today I will use zoom, alignment guides and resize handles, then change an object's colour or border.

## Guides line things up
A red **alignment guide** shows when objects line up. Blue **size guides** show when two objects are the same size.

![A red alignment guide between two shapes]({{res:l3-align-guide.png}})
![Blue size guides as a shape is resized]({{res:l3-resize-guide.png}})

## Use zoom
Put the steps to use zoom in order.

```order
Click the Zoom button
Click on the part you want to see bigger
Place your small detail
Click the arrow next to Zoom and choose Fit
```

## 🟢 Support
Drag each guide to what it tells you.

```sort
Red alignment guide: the objects are lined up
Blue size guide: the objects are the same size
```
