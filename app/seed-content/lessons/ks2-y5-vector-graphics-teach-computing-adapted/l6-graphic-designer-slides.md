## Design your logo
- choose what it is **for**
- add shapes, change colours and borders
- **group** the parts when it is done

> 🧑‍🏫 You-do. Support: a 2-shape logo. Core: use colour, border and grouping. Challenge: layer parts for depth. Nobody's screen looks less done.

## Vector or paint?
- **vector** = made from shapes you can move
- **paint** = made from **pixels** (coloured dots)
- a logo is best as a **vector**

> 🧑‍🏫 Likely error: thinking they are the same → fix-words "vector = shapes you can move; paint = dots you colour in." The worksheet sorts vector vs paint.
