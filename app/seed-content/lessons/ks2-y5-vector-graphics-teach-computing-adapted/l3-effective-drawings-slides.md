## Zoom in to add detail
- click **Zoom**, then click the small part
- the view gets bigger, not the object
- click the arrow by Zoom and choose **Fit** to go back

> 🧑‍🏫 I-do. Model zoom on a tiny eye. Stress: zoom changes the VIEW, not the size.

## Guides help you be neat
![A red alignment guide between two shapes]({{res:l3-align-guide.png}})

- a **red** line = the objects line up
- a **blue** line = the objects are the same size
- guides help your drawing look tidy

> 🧑‍🏫 We-do. Drag a shape slowly so the red guide appears. The worksheet sorts each guide to what it tells you.

## Resize to match
![Blue size guides as a shape is resized]({{res:l3-resize-guide.png}})

- drag a **corner handle** to resize
- watch the blue size guide
- stop when it matches the other shape

> 🧑‍🏫 Likely error: dragging a side handle squashes the shape → fix-words "corner handle keeps it the right shape."

## Change the look
![Circles with different fills and borders]({{res:l3-three-circles.png}})

- change the **fill** colour
- change the **border** colour
- change the border to **dashes**

> 🧑‍🏫 You-do. Support: zoom and place one detail. Core: build the picture with guides. Challenge: modify a border style and colour.
