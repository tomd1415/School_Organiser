## Remixing in Scratch
You will open a Scratch project that someone else made (the Mars Lander). You can:
- **See inside** — look at the blocks that make it work
- **Remix** — make your own copy to change

## Is it always OK to use someone's work?
Working online can be **public** (anyone can see and use it) or **private** (only some people can). Even when work is shared, you must follow the rules — you need permission, and you should say whose work it was. This is called **copyright**.
