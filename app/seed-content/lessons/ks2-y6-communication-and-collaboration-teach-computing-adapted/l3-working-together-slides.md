# Working together

## Today we are learning
- how to open and work on a file that is shared online
- the internet lets people share different media
- how to work with others online to make something together

> 🧑‍🏫 Read the three "I can…" aloud. Words on the board: collaborate · share · online · Chat · Explore. Recap: packets carry all kinds of data.

## Starter — working far apart
- people who are not together can still work together
- ways: a shared online file, email, a video call, a letter, travelling
- which ones use the internet?

> 🧑‍🏫 Pupils sort the ways on the starter worksheet. Draw out that a shared online file lets two people work on the SAME thing at once.

## A shared file online
![The Explore panel in a shared slide deck]({{res:l3-explore-panel.png}})

- your whole group opens the same slide deck
- Explore = find pictures and facts
- Chat = message your group

> 🧑‍🏫 Demonstrate opening the shared deck, the Explore panel, and Chat. Each group has its own country. Only share each deck with that group.

## Your turn — a country fact file
- work with your group, but stay at your own computer
- add text and a picture to your slides
- use Chat to talk to your group

> 🧑‍🏫 Pupils order the "add a picture" steps on the activity worksheet. TA: prompt with the Explore button; do not make the slide for them. Movement break is built in.

## What did we share?
- text, images — maybe video and sound
- all shared over the internet, in packets

> 🧑‍🏫 Ask: could you have shared all of that by phone? (You can talk, but not share files.) Support tick the media; Challenge: what was harder online?

## I can…
Tick your three "I can…". Tell me one tool you used to work with your group.
