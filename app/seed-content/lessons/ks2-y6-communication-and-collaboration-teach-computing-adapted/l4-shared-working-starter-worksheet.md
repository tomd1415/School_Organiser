## What we are learning
Today I will learn how people online can use and change work that someone else has made.

## Building on someone else's work
You start a drawing. You pass it to a partner. They add to it and make it better. You have **reused** and changed someone else's work.

Computers let us do this online. You can take someone's project and make your own version of it. This is called a **remix**.
