## What we are learning
Today I will learn the many different ways people communicate, and how the internet helps.

## So many ways to say one thing
There are lots of ways to communicate the same message. Think about how many ways you could tell someone "**Yes**" — you could nod, give a thumbs up, say the word, or write it down.

## When we are far apart
Some ways only work when you are in the same room (like a nod). When people are far apart, they use the internet to communicate.
