## What we are learning
Today I will learn that data travels across the internet in small parts called packets.

## Big data, small parts
When a computer sends data over the internet, it does **not** send it all in one go. It breaks the data into small parts. Each small part is called a **packet**.

![The internet connects computers around the world]({{res:l2-internet-globe.png}})

Small parts are easier and quicker to send across the network.
