## What we are learning

## The toolbar
The font box shows the name of the font. Click the arrow next to it to choose a new font.

## Change the font
Drag the steps into the right order.

```order
Click and drag across all the text to select it
Click the arrow next to the font box
Choose a new font from the list
```
