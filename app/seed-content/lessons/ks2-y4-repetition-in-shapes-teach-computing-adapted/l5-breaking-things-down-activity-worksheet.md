## What we are learning
Today I will make a **procedure** in Logo and call it to draw a pattern.

## What is a procedure?
A **procedure** is a named chunk of code you can run again and again. You make it once, give it a name, then **call** it by typing the name.

![The Logo procedure editor: to square / repeat 4 [fd 100 rt 90] / end]({{res:l5-logo-procedure-editor-to-square-repeat-end-teach-computing.png}})

A procedure starts with `to` and a name, has the commands in the middle, and finishes with `end`.
