## What we are learning
Today I will type Logo commands and watch the turtle draw a line.

## Starter — what is a turtle?
In Logo, the **turtle** is the little arrow on the screen. When you type a command, the turtle moves and draws a line, like a pen.

| Question | Choose |
|---|---|
| What does the turtle do when you type a command? | (  ) it moves and draws a line (  ) it makes a sound (  ) nothing |
