## 🟡 Core
The word **repeat** means to do something again.

| Question | Choose |
|---|---|

## 🔴 Challenge
Sort each thing into whether it has a **repeating pattern** or **no pattern**.

```sort
Repeats: stripes on a zebra, days of the week, tiles on a floor
No pattern: a pile of leaves, splashes of paint
```
