## What we are learning
Today I will choose which numbers to change in a loop to draw different shapes.

## The shapes table
![A table of shapes with their angles: triangle 120, pentagon 72, hexagon 60, octagon 45, decagon 36]({{res:l4-regular-shapes-with-their-turn-angles-and-loop-code-teach-computing.png}})

Every shape uses the same pattern: `repeat (sides) [FD 100 RT (angle)]`. You change **two numbers**: the number of sides and the turn angle.

| Question | Choose |
