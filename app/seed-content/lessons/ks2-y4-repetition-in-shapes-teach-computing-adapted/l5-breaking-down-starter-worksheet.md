## What we are learning
Today I will break a big task into small steps. Breaking down is called **decompose**.

## Starter — what does decompose mean?
**Decompose** means to break something down into smaller parts. A big job like "get ready for PE" is really lots of smaller steps.

| Question | Choose |
|---|---|
| What does "decompose" mean? | (  ) break into smaller parts (  ) make bigger (  ) delete it |
