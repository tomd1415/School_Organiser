## Data and information
- **Data** = raw facts with no meaning yet (e.g. 14, blue, tent).
- **Information** = data that has meaning (e.g. "this customer bought a tent").

> 🧑‍🏫 I-do: give an example. We-do: pupils match the two words on the starter worksheet. Fix-words: "information is data that has MEANING."

## The Data Protection Act
The law says companies must keep your data **safe** and use it **responsibly**.
