# Rise of the bots — malware

## Today we are learning
- list the common types of malware
- match each type of malware to what it does
- explain how a virus, worm and trojan are different
- explain how malicious bots can affect society

## Starter — your files are locked

## Bots and society
![An adware attack card]({{res:l4-attack-adware-card.jpg}})

A **bot** is an automated account. A **botnet** is a network of them.
