## Sort the defences

```sort
Stops malware: anti-malware, anti-virus, upgrade the software
Stops weak passwords being cracked: two-factor authentication, strong password rules, CAPTCHA
Stops too much traffic: firewall, pay the ISP to limit traffic
```
