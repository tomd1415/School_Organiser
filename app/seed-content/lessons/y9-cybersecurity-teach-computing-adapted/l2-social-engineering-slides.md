## The tricks
- **Phishing** — a fake message with a link, to steal your details.
- **Blagging** — a made-up story to win your trust.
- **Shouldering** — looking over your shoulder as you type.

> 🧑‍🏫 I-do: define each with the slide example. We-do: pupils match trick → meaning on the worksheet. Fix-words: "phishing = a fake MESSAGE; blagging = a made-up STORY."
