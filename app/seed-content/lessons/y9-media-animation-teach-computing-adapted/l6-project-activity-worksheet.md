## What we are learning
Today I will bring together my skills to make and render a 3–10 second animation.

## Remember your skills
Sort each skill into the right group.

```sort
Modelling: extrude, loop cut, knife tool
Animation: keyframes, timeline, parenting
Lights and camera: spotlight, rule of thirds, f-stop
```

## The order of work
Put the steps to make your film in the right order.

```order
Make your models
Name and parent your objects
Add keyframes to animate them
Set up the lights and camera
Render your animation to a video file
```
