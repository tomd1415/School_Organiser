# Computer networks (part 2) — summative assessment

## Today we are learning
- show what I have learned across the whole unit in a final quiz

> 🧑‍🏫 This is the assessment lesson — no new content. Read the four "I can…" aloud so pupils know what is being checked. Keep the room calm and quiet; this is a low-stakes check, not a high-pressure exam.

## What the quiz covers
- hosting and the cloud
- protocols and what they are used for
- the TCP/IP and OSI models
- network security

> 🧑‍🏫 Hand out the end-of-unit quiz. Reassure: every question links to something we have done. Read any question aloud on request.

## How to work
- Work on your own.
- Choose the best answer for each question.
- If you are stuck, move on and come back.

> 🧑‍🏫 Allow the full lesson. Offer movement breaks as normal. The closed questions can be self- or teacher-marked afterwards using the answer key in the conversion notes.

## When you finish
- Check your answers.
- Rate how confident you feel.
- Take a photo of your finished quiz.

> 🧑‍🏫 Plenary: ask a few pupils which topic they feel strongest on. Note who needs another look at the layer models.
