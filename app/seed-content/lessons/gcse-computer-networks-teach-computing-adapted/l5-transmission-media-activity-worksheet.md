## What we are learning
Today I will define wired and wireless transmission media, describe copper, fibre, WiFi and Bluetooth, and choose the right one for a scenario.

**Transmission media** is just *how* the data travels. **Wired** uses a physical cable. **Wireless** sends signals through the air.

![A network cable with an RJ45 connector — an example of wired media]({{res:l5-ethernet-cable.png}})
