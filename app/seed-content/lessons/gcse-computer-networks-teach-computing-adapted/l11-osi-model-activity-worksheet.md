## The seven layers, top to bottom
The OSI model splits network communication into seven layers. Drag them into the right order, from the top layer to the bottom layer.

```order
Application — talks directly to the app the user is using.
Presentation — puts data in the right format; may encrypt or decrypt it.
Session — sets up and keeps the connection going.
Transport — breaks data into segments.
Network — makes IP packets with source and destination addresses.
Data link — breaks packets into frames and deals with MAC addresses.
Physical — sends the bits as light, electricity or radio waves.
```
