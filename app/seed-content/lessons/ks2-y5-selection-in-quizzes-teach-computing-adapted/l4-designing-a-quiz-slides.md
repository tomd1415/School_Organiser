## Plan your questions
- pick your topic (maths, animals, football…)
- write each question and its correct answer
- decide the outcome for correct and for wrong

> 🧑‍🏫 I-do: model one row on the design sheet. Support: give a topic. TA: prompt, do not do it for them.

## Which outcome?
- the user types an answer
- the answer is checked against the condition
- if it matches → TRUE outcome; if not → FALSE outcome

> 🧑‍🏫 We-do: follow given answers (6, 11, 24, 4) through a plan and choose the outcome each time.
