## What we are learning
Today I will make a chart, read it, and choose when to use a table or a chart.

## Making a chart
You make a chart from data in your spreadsheet. Put the steps in order.

```order
Highlight the cells you want to chart
Click Insert
Click Chart
Choose the chart type you want, such as a pie chart
```

## Table or chart?
Sometimes a table is best, and sometimes a chart is best. Sort each job into the right group.

```sort
Use a table when: you want to see every exact figure, you want to do calculations
Use a chart when: you want to compare amounts quickly, you want a picture to share
```
