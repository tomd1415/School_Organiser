## What we are learning
Today I will use formulas to work out my party costs, and answer a question with my spreadsheet.

## Working out the costs
Your spreadsheet already has headings. You enter your choices, then use formulas to work out the costs:
- a **subtotal** is the cost of one item: cost × quantity
- the **total budget** is: attendees × budget per person
- the **party cost per person** is: total party cost ÷ attendees
