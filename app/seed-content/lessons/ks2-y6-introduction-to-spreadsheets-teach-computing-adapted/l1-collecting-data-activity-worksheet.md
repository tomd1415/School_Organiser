## Giving your data a structure
A pile of numbers is hard to read. We give data a **structure** by putting it in a **table** with **headings** at the top of each column. Headings tell you what each number means.

Here is a table that has clear headings (the words along the top): Name, Roll 1, Roll 2, Roll 3, Roll 4, Roll 5, Total.

## Typing data into a spreadsheet
A **spreadsheet** is a computer program for storing data in a grid of boxes. Here is data typed into a spreadsheet. Each box holds one piece of data.

![Data in a spreadsheet grid]({{res:l1-spreadsheet-grid.png}})

Put the steps in order. How do you get your data into a spreadsheet?

```order
Collect your data
Put the data in a table with headings
Open a new spreadsheet
Type the headings along the top
Type your data under the headings
```
