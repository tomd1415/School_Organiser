## Why organise data?
- it is easier to read
- it is easier to understand
- it is easier to do calculations with

> 🧑‍🏫 Pupils tick what a spreadsheet helps with on the starter. Support pupils choose why we organise data.

## Working out the costs
![A completed party spreadsheet with a chart]({{res:l5-party-chart.png}})

- subtotal = cost × quantity
- total budget = attendees × budget per person
- party cost per person = total party cost ÷ attendees

> 🧑‍🏫 Model =G2 to copy the attendees, then cost × quantity. Pupils match the calculations, then build their sheet and screenshot it. TA: prompt the formula, do not type it for them.
