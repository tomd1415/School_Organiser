## Functions work on a range of cells
A **function** is a ready-made formula. **SUM** adds up many cells at once. A group of cells together is a **range**, written like **B2:G2** (from B2 to G2). You find functions with the **∑ (sigma)** button.

To total the scores in row 2, the formula is **=SUM(B2:G2)**.

![A spreadsheet of scores with totals]({{res:l4-times-table-totals.png}})

Put the steps in order. How do you total a row with SUM?

```order
Click the cell where you want the total
Click the ∑ (sigma) button and choose SUM
Drag to select the range of cells, like B2 to G2
Press Enter to see the total
Drag the cell down to duplicate the formula for the other rows
```
