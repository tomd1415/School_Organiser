## What is a representation?
Agreed symbols that hold information — words, numbers, flags, dots, 0s and 1s.

![Binary representations concept map]({{res:l1-binary-representations-concept-map.jpg}})

> 🧑‍🏫 Likely error: "a representation is only a picture." Fix-words: "ANY agreed symbols — including the 0s and 1s a computer uses."

## Museum visit — store, communicate, process
Old writing did three jobs: it stored, communicated and processed information.

> 🧑‍🏫 Card-sort on the worksheet: store / communicate / process. Support sorts cards; Core writes the three verbs; Challenge gives one example of each.

## Choosing representations
Who decides what symbols mean? We pick the form that suits the task.

> 🧑‍🏫 We-do → you-do. Prompt "what is it FOR?" — a map vs written directions. Do not give the answer.

## Meaning
