# Lights and drums

## Today we are learning
- name some coding schemes
- measure a message by counting symbols
- see how symbols ride on light, sound and paper

## Lights and drums — your task
Encode → transmit → receive → decode a short message in your group.

![Talking drums carry a message as sound]({{res:l2-talking-drums.jpg}})

> 🧑‍🏫 Each group gets a message, a coding scheme and equipment. Support follows the order strip; Core encodes + decodes; Challenge also counts the length. Keep drums soft, torches steady.

## A symbol vs how it is carried
The dot is the symbol. The torch flash is just how we carry it.

> 🧑‍🏫 Likely error: confusing the symbol with its medium. Fix-words: "the dot is the symbol; the flash, the beat, the dot on paper just carry it."

## Length = number of symbols
A longer message has more symbols. Encoded messages are usually longer.

> 🧑‍🏫 Core question: count the symbols in HELLO. Connect to L3 next lesson (bits).
