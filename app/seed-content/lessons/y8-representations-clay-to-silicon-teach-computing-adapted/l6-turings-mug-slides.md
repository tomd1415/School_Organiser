## Part 2 — the puzzle
- ◯ = 0, ⬤ = 1
- patterns → binary → decimal → letters → a word
- the numbers by the word → binary → the lock combination

> 🧑‍🏫 Likely error: maps letters wrong (A = 0). Fix-words: "A is 1, B is 2 … letter number = place in the alphabet." If a number is over 26 or the letters do not spell a word, try the other reading — do not give the word.
