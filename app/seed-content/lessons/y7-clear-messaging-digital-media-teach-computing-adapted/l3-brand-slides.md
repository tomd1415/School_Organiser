# Brand

## Today we are learning
- recolour a logo in a graphics program
- combine text and a logo on a slide
- give feedback using comments

> 🧑‍🏫 Read the three "I can…" aloud. Vocabulary on board: brand · logo · slide · comment.

## Starter — what brand is this?
![A cube made of social media logos]({{res:l3-social-media-brand-cube-teach-computing.jpg}})

What tells you which brands these are?

> 🧑‍🏫 Draw out: logo shape, colours, the symbol/letter. Pupils answer the starter worksheet. Link to online safety on social media.

## Choose and recolour a logo  (I do)
Pick one logo. Use the fill tool to recolour it. Save it with a new name.

![Eye logo]({{res:l3-logo-eye-teach-computing.png}}) ![Laptop logo]({{res:l3-logo-laptop-teach-computing.png}}) ![Webcam logo]({{res:l3-logo-webcam-teach-computing.png}})

> 🧑‍🏫 Keep it simple — only the fill tool. The skill is moving BETWEEN apps, not fancy art. Likely error: forgets to save with a new name → fix-words "Save As, new name".

## Build your slide  (we do)
Set the background colour. Add your logo, the charity name, and a key message.

> 🧑‍🏫 Choose a SIMPLE template so the design choices are theirs. Match background colour to their poster. Support: colour codes from last lesson.

## Your turn — feedback  (you do)
Swap with a partner. Add one positive comment and one improvement.

> 🧑‍🏫 Model kind, specific feedback. Pupils screenshot their slide on the activity worksheet.
