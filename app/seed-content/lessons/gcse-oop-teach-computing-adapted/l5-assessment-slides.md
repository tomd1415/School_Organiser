## Part 2 — adding to the library

> 🧑‍🏫 Exam conditions, but they may use earlier programs (Pet, Monster, Friend, Enemy) as inspiration. Not everyone will finish — weight Part 1 more. Use the rubric to mark the code.

## Recap — what is OOP?
OOP is a paradigm. Classes hold data (attributes) and actions (methods). Objects are made from classes. Subclasses inherit from superclasses.

> 🧑‍🏫 Final recap. Ask pupils to define each term in turn. Close the unit calmly.
