## Starter — plan your monster
You are a game programmer on a game called **Monster Quest**.
Every monster needs: a **name**, **health points**, and a **line of dialogue**.
Every monster must be able to: **take damage** and **speak**.

## Attribute or method?
Drag each part of the Monster into the right group.

```sort
Attributes (data): name, health, speech
Methods (actions): take_damage, speak
```
