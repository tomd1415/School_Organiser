# Making careful choices

## Today we are learning
- choose the right shape for the job
- choose colours for my picture
- build a picture from shapes
- make a picture in the style of an artist

> 🧑‍🏫 Read the four "I can…" aloud. Vocabulary: shape tool · fill tool · Henri Matisse · collage. Same routine.

## The artist — Henri Matisse
![A photo of the artist Henri Matisse]({{res:l3-matisse-portrait.png}})

- Henri Matisse was an artist
- he cut out shapes and stuck them down — a collage
- he used bright colours

> 🧑‍🏫 Show "The Snail" by Matisse on the board (link from the original deck — copyright, not embedded). Point out the bright shapes that spiral round.

## The tools we need
- shape tool — makes circles and squares
- fill tool — colours a shape
- undo tool — fixes a mistake

> 🧑‍🏫 Ask which tools are best for a shape picture. The spray can is not needed. The activity worksheet sorts good choices from bad ones.

## I do — build with shapes
- draw a bright square to start
- add big shapes in each corner
- fill them with bright colours

> 🧑‍🏫 Model it slowly. Likely error: shape filled with the wrong colour → fix-words "press undo, pick the colour, fill again."

## Your turn
- choose your shapes
- choose bright colours
- build your own shape picture

> 🧑‍🏫 You-do. Support: a few shapes ready to fill. Challenge: combine line and shape tools. TA: prompt, do not do it for them. Screenshot onto the worksheet.

## I can…
Tick your four "I can…". Show me your favourite shape.

> 🧑‍🏫 Note who can choose a shape and a colour on purpose.
