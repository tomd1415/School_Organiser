## What we are learning
Today I will paint a sunflower on paper, then say what is different from the computer.

## Computer or paper?
Sort each thing into where it belongs.

```sort
Painting on a computer: Use the undo button to fix a mistake, Lines can be very neat, You need a mouse or screen
Painting on paper: Use a brush and real paint, Your hands can get messy, You cannot press undo
```
