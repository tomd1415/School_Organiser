## What we are learning
Today I will make a picture in the style of Piet Mondrian using squares, lines and fill.

## The steps
Put the steps for a Mondrian picture in order. Drag them into the right order.

```order
Draw a big square
Add straight lines to make boxes
Fill some boxes with red, yellow or blue
Leave some boxes white
```
