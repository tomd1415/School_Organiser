## What we are learning
Today I will give my partner clear instructions to move, one at a time.

## We do — be the robot
One person is the robot. The other gives one instruction at a time. The robot only does what it is told.

## 🟢 Support
Put these instructions in an order to send your partner across the room. Drag them into order.

```order
Stand up
Turn to face the door
Step
Step
Stop
```

## 🟡 Core
| Question | Choose |
