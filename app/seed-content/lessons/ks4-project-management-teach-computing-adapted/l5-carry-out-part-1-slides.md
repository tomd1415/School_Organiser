## Starter — formulas and functions
- recap what =SUM, =MIN, =A4*B4 and =IF do

> 🧑‍🏫 Pupils explain each on the starter worksheet, then peer-mark.

## What is an integrated system?
- one **workbook** = one file with many **sheets**
- all the data in one place, instead of separate files

> 🧑‍🏫 We are now at the EXECUTE stage of the project life cycle.

## Formula or function?
- a **formula** is your own calculation: =A4*B4
- a **function** is a built-in tool with a name: =SUM, =IF

> 🧑‍🏫 Likely error: forgetting the = at the start. Fix-words: "every formula starts with =."

## Build and format
- open your copy of the workbook template
- format it to match your poster **house style**
- add the formulas and functions, one sheet at a time

> 🧑‍🏫 Use the step-by-step guide. Keep the pace up. TA: prompt, do not do it for them.

## Check it
- after each sheet, check the numbers look right
- screenshot your workbook onto the activity worksheet

> 🧑‍🏫 Testing in full comes in lesson 7, but a quick check now saves time.
