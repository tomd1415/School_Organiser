## From aims to objectives
- an **aim** = a big thing the project must do
- **objectives** = the smaller tasks that get you there

> 🧑‍🏫 Worked example: aim "make the integrated system" → objectives like "make a stock sheet", "add a profit formula".

## SMART goals
- **S**pecific — says exactly what to do
- **M**easurable — you can check it
- **A**chievable — you can do it
- **R**ealistic — it fits this project
- **T**imely — it has a deadline

> 🧑‍🏫 Likely error: a vague goal like "do the spreadsheet". Fix-words: "say WHAT, and by WHEN."

## Interaction
- when one stage **flows into** the next
