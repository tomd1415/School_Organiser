## What we are learning
Today I will build the Delicious Desserts workbook and add formulas and functions to it.

## What is an integrated system?
A **workbook** is one file with many **sheets**, all in one place. The dessert shop keeps stock, costs and sales sheets together in one workbook, instead of separate files.

## Formula or function?
Drag each one into the right group. A **formula** is your own calculation. A **function** is a built-in tool with a name.

```sort
A formula (your own calculation): =A4*B4, =E5-D5
A function (a built-in tool): =SUM(A1:A9), =IF(E5>D5,"Yes","No")
```
