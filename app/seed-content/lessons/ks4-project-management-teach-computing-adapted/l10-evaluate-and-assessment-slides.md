## Today we are learning
- evaluate a completed project
- explain iteration and interaction

## Starter — iteration and interaction
- iteration = repeating a stage to improve it
- interaction = one stage flowing into the next

> 🧑‍🏫 Pupils defined these in lesson 3. Self-mark from the board.

## The final evaluation
- scope (what the project was about)
- each stage: what you did and how well it went
- recommendations: what to improve next time

> 🧑‍🏫 Pupils need all their documents and products open. 35 minutes of calm, independent writing. TA: prompt, do not do it for them.
