## Steps — draw a shape and colour it
Drag these steps into the right order.

```order
Choose the rectangle tool.
Click and drag on the canvas to draw the shape.
Choose the selection tool (the black arrow).
Click the shape to select it.
Open Fill and Stroke and pick a fill colour.
```
