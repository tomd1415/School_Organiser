## What we are learning
Today I will compare vector and bitmap images, decide which suits which use, and evaluate my project.

## Vector or bitmap?
The same fish as a vector graphic and as a real photograph (a bitmap).

![A vector graphic of a clownfish]({{res:l6-vector-clownfish-teach-computing.png}})

![A photograph of a clownfish — a bitmap]({{res:l6-photo-clownfish-teach-computing.jpg}})

A bitmap is made of tiny squares of colour called **pixels**. Zoom in and you can see them.

![A photo zoomed in to show pixels]({{res:l6-bitmap-pixels-butterfly-teach-computing.png}})

## Which type suits which use?
Drag each use into the right column.

```sort
Best as a vector: a company logo, a road sign, an app icon, a simple cartoon

```
