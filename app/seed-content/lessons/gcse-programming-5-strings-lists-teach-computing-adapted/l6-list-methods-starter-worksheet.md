## What we are learning
Today I will **traverse** (go through) a list with a for loop, and use **list methods** like sort and reverse.

## Starter — predict this program
You can loop over a list just like you loop over a string.

```python
animals = ["cat", "dog", "fox"]
for animal in animals:
    print(animal)
```
