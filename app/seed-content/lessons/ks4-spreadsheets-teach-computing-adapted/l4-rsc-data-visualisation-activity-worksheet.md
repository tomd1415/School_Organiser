## What we are learning
Today I will make a column chart and a pie chart, give them clear titles and labels, and choose the right chart for the data.

## Two charts, two jobs
A column chart compares amounts. A pie chart shows parts of a whole.

## Sort — which chart for which job?
Drag each job into the right group.

```sort
Column chart: compare votes week by week, compare income from each seat type
Pie chart: show each section as a slice of all seats sold, show how a budget is split
```

## Build a chart (drag the steps into order)
Drag the steps into the correct order to make a column chart.

```order
Highlight the cells with the data
Click Insert
Choose Column chart
Change the title so it describes the data
Move the chart to its own sheet
```
