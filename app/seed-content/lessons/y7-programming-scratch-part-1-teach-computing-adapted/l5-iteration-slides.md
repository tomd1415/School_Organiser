## What is iteration?
**Iteration** is repeating instructions. A **count-controlled** loop repeats a set number of times (`repeat 10`).

> 🧑‍🏫 Real example: writing lines as a punishment — "do this 10 times". Computers repeat fast and exactly. Count-controlled = we know how many times.
