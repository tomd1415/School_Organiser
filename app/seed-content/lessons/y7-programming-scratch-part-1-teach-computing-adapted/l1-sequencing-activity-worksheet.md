## What we are learning

## Order the song
*Frère Jacques* has eight lines. Each line is sung **twice** before the next. Drag the lines into the order they are sung.

```parsons
Frère Jacques
Frère Jacques
Dormez-vous?
Dormez-vous?
Sonnez les matines
Sonnez les matines
Ding ding dong
Ding ding dong
```
