# Sequencing in Scratch

## Today we are learning
- computers need precise instructions
- what a sequence is
- predict what a sequence will do
- put music blocks in the right order

> 🧑‍🏫 Read the four "I can…" aloud. Same routine as every lesson. Vocabulary on board: sequence · instruction · execute · subroutine.

## Starter — robots are not like people
![People building a robot]({{res:l1-robot-precise-instructions-teach-computing.png}})

A computer does exactly what it is told. It does not guess.

> 🧑‍🏫 Think · pair · share: "Can a computer do a musician's job?" Steer to: a computer follows the sequence exactly but cannot add its own flair. Pupils answer the choice on the starter worksheet.

## Watch — what is a command?
[▶ Play the commands video]({{res:l1-commands-video-teach-computing.mp4}})

> 🧑‍🏫 Teacher-played hook (has motion + sound). Short clip showing single commands. Pause and ask "what is the very first instruction?"

## A sequence is order
![A music score, a recipe, furniture instructions]({{res:l1-sequencing-examples-lego-teach-computing.png}})

A **sequence** is a set of instructions carried out **in order**, one after another.

> 🧑‍🏫 Other sequences: music score, recipe, flat-pack furniture. Ask the class for one more. Key word: execute = the computer carries out the instruction.

## Predict — Frère Jacques  (I do)
Listen to the song, then predict what the program will play.

> 🧑‍🏫 Play the Frère Jacques audio (shared area). Stress: there is no wrong prediction. Each line of the song is sung twice.

## Your turn — order the blocks  (you do)
Drag the music blocks into the correct order on your worksheet, then build it in Scratch.

> 🧑‍🏫 Support: pupils have the first line placed for them — match the rest. Challenge: put each line into a subroutine. Likely error: blocks in the wrong order → fix-words "click one block to hear it, then check it comes next."

## I can…
Tick your four "I can…". Play me your song.

> 🧑‍🏫 Note pairs who got a working sequence. Plenary "sequence the sounds" can run live if time.
