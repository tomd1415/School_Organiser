## What we are learning
Today I will work with my group on a shared online file and share different media.

## A shared file online
Your group will all open the **same** slide deck at the same time, from different computers. You will work together to make a fact file about a country.

The **Explore** tool helps you find pictures and facts. You can put a picture straight onto your slide from there. The **Chat** tool lets you message your group.

![The Explore panel in a shared slide deck]({{res:l3-explore-panel-in-a-shared-slide-deck-teach-computing.png}})

## Add a picture to your slide
Put the steps in order.

```order
Open the Explore tool
Search for what you want (for example, your country)
Choose a picture you like
Add the picture to your slide
```
