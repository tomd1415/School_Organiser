## What we are learning
Today I will compare ways of communicating online, decide what is safe to share, and know what to do if I see something upsetting.

## Comparing ways to communicate
We can compare ways to communicate online. Two useful questions are:
- **Public or private?** Who can see it?
- **One-to-one or one-to-many?** Are you talking to one person or many?

## Sort: public or private

## If you see something upsetting online
If you ever see something online that upsets you or is not right, put these steps in order.

```order
Stop and do not share it with anyone
Turn off the screen or minimise the page
Tell a trusted adult straight away
Together, report it
```
