## What we are learning

## Working together over a distance
Sometimes people need to work together but they are not in the same room — they might be in different towns, or even different countries.

Here are some ways people who are far apart could work together. Sort them into ways that use a computer and the internet, and ways that do not.

```sort
Uses the internet: share an online file, send an email, have a video call
Does not use the internet: travel to meet in person, post a letter
```
