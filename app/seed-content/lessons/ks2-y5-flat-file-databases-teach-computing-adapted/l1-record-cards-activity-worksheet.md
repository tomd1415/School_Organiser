## What we are learning
Today I will fill in record cards, sort them into order, and group them to answer a question.

## A record card
A computer database holds the same kind of cards. This one card is a **record**. The boxes around it are **fields** (one fact each).

![One record from an animal database, showing fields]({{res:l1-minibeast-card.png}})

## Make your cards
Choose 8 animals. On each card, write the animal name in the middle and tick the fields that are true: Can fly · Has six legs · Lives in large groups · Is more than one colour.

## Sort the facts
Drag each one into the right box.

```sort
Field (one fact): Can fly, Has six legs, Lives in large groups
Record (a whole card): The bat card, The wasp card, The cat card
```

## 🟡 Core
Put the steps to sort your cards into A–Z order. Drag them into order.

```order
Mix up all your cards
Find the name that starts nearest to A
Put that card first
Find the next name in order
Carry on until you reach Z
```
