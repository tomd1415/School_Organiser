## What we are learning
Today I will change parts of a loop in a game and re-use code on a new sprite. **Keep the loop — change what is inside it.**

## What can I change in a loop?
Inside a forever loop you can change **the number**, **the block**, or **the sprite** it is on.

![The shark game]({{res:l4-shark-game.png}})

## Sort the changes
A bigger move number makes a sprite faster. A smaller number makes it slower. Drag each change into the right group.

```sort
Makes it faster: change x by 10 instead of 5, move 20 steps instead of 10
Makes it slower: change x by 2 instead of 10, add a longer wait
```
