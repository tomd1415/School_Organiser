## What we are learning
Today I will make music about an idea, using high and low sounds.

## Pictures and sounds
Music can make a picture in your head. Some sounds feel high. Some sounds feel low.

![A child's drawing of music]({{res:l3-music-drawing.jpg}})

## 🟢 Support
Sort each sound. Is it a **high** sound or a **low** sound?

```sort
High sound (draw near the top): a tiny bird, a squeak, a whistle
Low sound (draw near the bottom): a big drum, a lion roar, a rumble
```

## 🟡 Core
Put the steps in order to make music in the Kandinsky tool.

```order
Open the Kandinsky tool
Draw a line or a shape
Press play to hear the sound
Try a line near the top for a high sound
```
