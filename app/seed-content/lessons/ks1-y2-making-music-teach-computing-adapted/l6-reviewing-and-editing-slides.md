# Reviewing and editing music

## Today we are learning
- open my saved work
- listen to and review my work
- edit my work to make it better
- say how I changed my work

> 🧑‍🏫 Read the four "I can…" aloud. Vocabulary on the board: open · review · edit · change · improve. Same routine as every lesson.

## Open your work
![Colourful music bars]({{res:l6-music-equaliser.png}})

- last lesson you saved a link
- open the link to get your music back
- this is one good thing about computers

> 🧑‍🏫 Help pupils open their saved links. Have the links ready in a shared place next to each name in case a pupil lost theirs.

## Review your work
- listen to your music
- think about your animal
- does it still sound like your animal?

> 🧑‍🏫 Review means listen and think. Ask pupils to listen once before changing anything. Likely error: changing lots at once → fix-words "change one thing, then listen."

## Edit your work
- add or remove notes
- try a different instrument or tempo
- use Undo to go back

> 🧑‍🏫 I-do then you-do. Model one small edit and an Undo. The worksheet asks pupils to tick what they can edit and to order the edit-and-save steps. Support: tick what you can change. Challenge: say what they changed and why it is better.

## Save again
- saving makes a NEW link
- keep the new link
- paste it next to your name

> 🧑‍🏫 Remind pupils Song Maker makes a new link each save. Keep both links if you want to compare before and after.

## Share and listen
- listen to a partner's music
- say one thing you like about it
- be kind

> 🧑‍🏫 Plenary. Pupils share and self-tick the four "I can…". Keep volume low — offer headphones. Movement break to finish the unit.
