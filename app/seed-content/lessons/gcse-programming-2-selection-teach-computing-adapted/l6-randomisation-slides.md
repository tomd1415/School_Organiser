## True random vs pseudorandom
- **True random** — from the physical world (atmospheric noise, lightning).
- **Pseudorandom** — made by a computer with maths (games, the `random` module).

> 🧑‍🏫 Pupils sort the cards on the worksheet. Likely error: thinking the computer's numbers are "truly" random — they are predictable to a computer, hence *pseudo*.
