## and / or — the playing card
Look at this card: the **5 of spades**. It is **black**, the value is **5** (odd), and the suit is **spades**.

![The five of spades playing card]({{res:l10-five-of-spades.png}})

- `and` is True only when **both** parts are True.
- `or` is True when **at least one** part is True.

Sort each statement about this card into True or False.

```sort
True: the card is black and the number is odd, the card is a spade or a heart, the value is less than 10 and it is black
False: the card is red or a club, the value is 5 and it is red, the card is a heart or a diamond
```
