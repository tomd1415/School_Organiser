Put the steps in the right order. Drag them into order.

```order

```

## Sharing data safely
Some data is fine to share. Some data is **private** and you should keep it safe. It is OK to say **no**. Sort each one.

```sort
OK to share: my favourite colour, my favourite fruit, my favourite animal
Keep private: my home address, my full date of birth, my password
```
