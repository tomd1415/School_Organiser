## A new way: block diagrams
![Car colours block diagram]({{res:l6-car-colours-block-diagram-teach-computing.png}})

- data shown as **blocks** in columns
- taller column = more
- it has **numbers** up the side

> 🧑‍🏫 Key idea. Likely error: reading at the wrong height → fix-words "follow the top of the column across to the number."

## Keeping data safe
- some data is fine to share (favourite colour)
- some data is **private** (address, birthday, password)
- it is OK to say **no**, and to tell an adult

> 🧑‍🏫 The worksheet sorts "OK to share" from "keep private". Reassure pupils they can always say no and report a worry.
