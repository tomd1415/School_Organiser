## What we are learning
Today I will make a tally chart and use it to compare totals.

## How a tally chart works
A tally chart keeps a record so you do not lose count. You make **one mark** for each object. When you get to **five** you draw a line across, like a **gate**. Then groups of five are easy to count.

Put the steps in the right order. Drag them into order.

```order
Count one object
Make one mark on the tally chart
At five, draw a line across to make a gate
Count up all the marks
Write the number in the Total box
```
