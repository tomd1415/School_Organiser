## What we are learning
Today I will turn my tally chart into a pictogram, then say what it shows.

## From tally chart to pictogram
First you collect data with a tally chart. Then you put that data into the computer to make a pictogram.

Put the steps in the right order. Drag them into order.

```order
Find a minibeast
Make one mark on the tally chart
Count up the totals
Open the Pictogram software
Add one picture for each one you tallied
```
