## What we are learning
Today I will learn that a condition is a sentence that can only be **true** or **false**. I will use a switch as an input.

## Sort the sentences
A **condition** can only be true or false. Drag each sentence into the right group.

```sort
This is a condition (true or false): It is raining, The door is open, It is past 6:30am
This is NOT a condition: What is the weather?, How are you?, What time is it?
```
