## What we are learning
Today I will design a carousel that uses selection. It must start when a switch is pressed and use at least two output devices.

## The project
![A fairground carousel]({{res:l5-carousel-photo.jpg}})

A carousel spins and lights up. Yours will use **selection**: if the switch is pressed, then the carousel starts.
