## What we are learning
Today I will learn about **selection**: IF a condition is true, THEN do an action.

## Sort: condition or action?
In "**if** it is raining, **then** put up your umbrella": the **condition** comes after "if"; the **action** comes after "then". Drag each phrase into the right group.

```sort
Condition (after "if"): It is raining, Button A is pressed, It is dark
Action (after "then"): Put up your umbrella, Flash the Sparkle, Turn on the light
```
