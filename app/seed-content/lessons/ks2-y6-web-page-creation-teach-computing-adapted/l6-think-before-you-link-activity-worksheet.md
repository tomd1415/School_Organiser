## What we are learning
Today I will explain the implications of linking to other people's work, add external links, and evaluate a website.

## Think before you link
An **implication** is something that might happen because of what you do. Before adding an **external link**, think about these:
- the page might be **deleted**, so the link will not work
- the **content may change**, and you can't control it
- the site might not be **secure**
- is the site **trustworthy**?
- **give credit** to the site you link to
- make links open in a new tab so users can **get back** to your site
