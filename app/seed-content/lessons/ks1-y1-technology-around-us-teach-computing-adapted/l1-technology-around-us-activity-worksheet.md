## What we are learning
Today I will sort things into technology and not technology, and say how technology helps us.

Technology is made by people to help us. A computer is technology.

![A desktop computer]({{res:l1-technology-computer.png}})

## Sort the items
Drag each thing into the right group. Ask yourself: "Did people make it? Does it help us?"

```sort
Technology: a computer, a pencil, scissors, a phone, a lamp
Not technology: a tree, a dog, a flower, a rock
```
