# Developing mouse skills

## Today we are learning
- use a mouse to open a program
- click and drag to make marks
- use a mouse to draw a picture

> 🧑‍🏫 Read the three "I can…" aloud. Vocabulary on the board: computer · mouse · trackpad · double-click. Same routine as every lesson.

## Mice can look different
- some mice have wires, some do not
- a trackpad can do the same job
- they all help you point and click

> 🧑‍🏫 Recap the parts of a computer from last lesson. Emphasise: different shapes, SAME job.

## The double-click
- a double-click is TWO clicks, fast
- it opens a program
- practise: clap twice, quickly

> 🧑‍🏫 Practise off-computer first: two fast claps or two taps on the leg. Check the speed. Then model on a real mouse. Likely error: clicks too slow / too far apart — fix-words "two fast clicks, close together."

## Open the paint program
- log in
- double-click the browser
- type the address and open the paint program

> 🧑‍🏫 This matches the ORDER task on the activity worksheet. Model opening the browser and typing the address (paintz.app). TA: prompt the steps, do not click for them.

## Draw with the mouse
- click and DRAG to make a line
- change the colour
- change how thick the line is

> 🧑‍🏫 Model the paint brush: click-drag makes a mark. Show colour and thickness. Then free time to draw a simple picture. They screenshot it. No need for detail.

## I can…
Tick your three "I can…". Show me your picture.

> 🧑‍🏫 Plenary: show an example. Ask which tool changed the picture (thicker line, new colour, fill).
