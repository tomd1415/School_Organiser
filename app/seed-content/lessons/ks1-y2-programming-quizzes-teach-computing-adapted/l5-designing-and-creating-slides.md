## Choose your pictures
![A space background with two sprites]({{res:l5-moon-aliens.jpg}})

- pick a **background** you like
- pick **sprites** that go with it
- write or draw your choice on the design sheet

> 🧑‍🏫 The starter checks the sprites match the background. Model one choice of your own.

## Write your algorithm
- an **algorithm** is the ordered steps
- tap → say "Yes" → do an action → change the background
- the order matters

> 🧑‍🏫 I-do then we-do. Write one algorithm on the board. Likely error: steps out of order → fix-words "read it like a list, top to bottom."

## Build it
- follow your own algorithm
- add one block at a time
- run it to check after each step

> 🧑‍🏫 You-do. Pupils build their quiz from their design. Support: build the right-answer sprite only. Challenge: both sprites.
