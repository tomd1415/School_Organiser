## What is an outcome?
- an **outcome** is what happens at the end
- clap your hands → the outcome is a **noise**
- pour juice and water → the outcome is a **drink**

> 🧑‍🏫 Use a real clap. The starter worksheet checks the idea of an outcome.

## Predict the outcome
- a program is a sequence of commands
- when you run it, there is an outcome
- look at the blocks and **predict** before you run it

> 🧑‍🏫 I-do. Show a short program, ask pupils to predict, then run it. Likely error: guessing without reading the blocks → fix-words "read the blocks in order, left to right."
