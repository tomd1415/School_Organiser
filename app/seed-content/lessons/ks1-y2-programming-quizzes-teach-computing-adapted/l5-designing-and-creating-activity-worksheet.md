## What we are learning
Today I will write my own algorithm and build my quiz.

## My algorithm
An **algorithm** is the ordered steps for your sprite. Put the steps for the right answer in order.

```order
Tap the sprite
The sprite says "Yes"
The sprite does an action, like a wiggle
The background changes to the next question
```

## Pick your actions
A sprite can do lots of actions. Sort these into the right group.

```sort
Moving actions: move up, move down, turn right
Other actions: say, grow, change page
```
