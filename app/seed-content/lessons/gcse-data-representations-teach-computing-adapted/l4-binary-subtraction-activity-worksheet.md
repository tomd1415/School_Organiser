## Borrowing — put the steps in order
When a column needs `0 - 1`, you must borrow. Drag these steps into order.

```order
Look at the column that needs 0 minus 1
Move left to the next column that has a 1
Borrow that 1 — it becomes two 1s because the place value halves
Now do the subtraction in the column you were stuck on
Carry on subtracting the rest of the columns
```

## Worked example
`1110 - 1101`: right column 0 - 1 needs a borrow. Borrow from the left, finish the columns. Answer = **1** (remove leading 0s). Check: 14 - 13 = 1. ✔
