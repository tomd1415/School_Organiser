## Overflow or underflow? — sort each meaning
Drag each phrase into the right error.

```sort
Overflow: the answer is too big to fit in the bits, a number grew too large
Underflow: the answer is too small or too close to zero, a tiny fraction is lost
```

## Worked example
`101 × 100`: ×100 means ×4, so shift **left 2 places**. `101` becomes `10100`. Check: 5 × 4 = 20. ✔
