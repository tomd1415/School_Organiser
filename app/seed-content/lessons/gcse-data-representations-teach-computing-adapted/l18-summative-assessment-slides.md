# Summative assessment

## Today we are learning
- show what I have learned across this unit in a calm, low-stakes quiz

> 🧑‍🏫 Read the four "I can…" aloud. Reassure pupils: every question links to something we covered. Hand out the end-of-unit quiz.

## Settle
- Read the four "I can…" at the bottom of the quiz.
- This is a check of what we have learned, not a high-pressure exam.

> 🧑‍🏫 Keep the room calm and quiet. Offer to read any question aloud. Movement breaks as normal.

## The quiz
- Work on your own.
- Part 1: choose the best answer. Part 2: tick all that are true. Part 3: a careers question.
- Then rate how confident you feel.

> 🧑‍🏫 The quiz is shared — everyone sits the same paper. The unit's differentiation lives in the per-lesson activity sheets. Read questions aloud on request.

## Finishing
- Rate your confidence on the slider.
- Take a photo of your finished quiz.

> 🧑‍🏫 A few pupils share which topic they feel strongest on. Mark closed questions with the answer key in the conversion notes.
