## What we are learning
Today I will learn three new techniques and film a short video from a storyboard.

## Three new techniques
- **static camera** — the camera stays still
- **zoom** — makes the subject bigger or smaller without moving the camera
- **pan and tilt** — the camera turns to follow, side to side or up and down
