## 🟡 Core — reshoot or edit?
Some problems you can fix by **editing**. Others you must **reshoot** (film again). Sort them.

```sort
Reshoot it: the camera pointed the wrong way, someone walked in front, the actor forgot the words
Fix by editing: there is a long boring bit at the start, the clips are in the wrong order, there is a wobbly second at the end
```
