# Importing and editing video

## Today we are learning
- import my video onto the computer
- find the tools in the video editor
- decide: reshoot or edit?
- split and trim a clip

> 🧑‍🏫 Read the four "I can…" aloud. Vocabulary on the board: import · clip · split · trim · reshoot · edit. First, give pupils time to film any scenes they still need.

## Get your video onto the computer
- **import** = copy your clips from the camera to the computer
- keep them in a **labelled folder** so you can find them
- each scene comes in as its own **clip**

> 🧑‍🏫 Activity 2. You may need a teacher account to import. Model finding and copying the files. Pupils order the import steps on the activity worksheet. Likely error: losing the files. Fix-words: "one labelled folder for everything."

## The video editor
![A film editor screen]({{res:l5-video-editor.png}})

- the **library** holds your clips
- the **preview** plays the video
- the **timeline** shows the clips in order
- the **tools** trim and split clips

> 🧑‍🏫 Pupils label the editor on the starter worksheet. Use whatever editor your school has (the picture is one example). Keep the screen calm — no auto-playing sound.

## Reshoot or edit?
![A film strip being cut with scissors]({{res:l5-trim-scissors.png}})

- **edit** the small problems (a boring bit, a wobble, wrong order)
- **reshoot** the big problems (wrong way, someone walked in, forgot the words)
- a **video producer** decides what to fix

> 🧑‍🏫 Activity 1. Pupils sort the problems on the activity worksheet. Discuss: editing is quicker, reshooting takes more time — choose wisely. TA: prompt, do not do it for them.

## Split and trim
- **split** cuts one clip into two parts
- **trim** cuts off the start or end of a clip
- watch the clip, THEN cut

> 🧑‍🏫 You-do. Model split then trim. Likely error: cutting before watching. Fix-words: "watch first, then cut." Pupils import their clips and try trimming one. Save before the end.

## I can…
Tick your four "I can…". Show your clips imported into the editor.

> 🧑‍🏫 Plenary. Check everyone has all their clips imported and saved, ready to finish next lesson.
