## What is a function?
A function is a ready-made tool that does a calculation. It starts with =.

> 🧑‍🏫 Likely error: forgets the brackets/range. Fix-words: "function name, then the range in brackets."

## SUM — add a range
= SUM(B2:B10) adds every number from B2 to B10.

> 🧑‍🏫 Model on the class data. Change the range to match your sheet.

## MAX and MIN — biggest and smallest
= MAX(B2:B10) finds the largest. = MIN(B2:B10) finds the smallest.

> 🧑‍🏫 We do: find the biggest donation together.

## COUNTA — count non-blank cells
= COUNTA(B2:B10) counts how many cells have something in them.

> 🧑‍🏫 Useful for "how many people answered".
