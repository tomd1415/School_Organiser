## What we are learning
Today I will sort input and output devices, and design my own digital device.

## Input or output? — sort it
- an **input** sends information IN (you give it to the device)
- an **output** sends information OUT (the device gives it to you)

![A screen is an output]({{res:l2-screen.png}})

```sort
Input: keyboard, mouse, microphone, button
Output: screen, speaker, printer, light
```
