## What we are learning
Today I will show how a message goes from one computer to another, and say why a network switch helps.

## A network
A **network** is many computers connected together so they can share information.

![A network of connected dots]({{res:l4-network.png}})

## A switch helps
Instead of joining every computer to every other computer, we join them all to one **network switch** in the middle. The switch passes the message to the right computer.

![A network switch]({{res:l4-switch.png}})

## Order — how a message travels
A message goes from computer 1 to computer 2 through the switch. Drag the steps into order.

```order
Computer 1 sends the message to the switch
The switch sees who the message is for
The switch passes the message to computer 2
Computer 2 receives the message
```
