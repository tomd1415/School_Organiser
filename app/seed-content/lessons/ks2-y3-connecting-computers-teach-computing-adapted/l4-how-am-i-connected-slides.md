# How am I connected?

## Today we are learning
- say what a connection is
- say what a network is
- show how a message goes between computers
- say why a network switch helps

> 🧑‍🏫 Read the four "I can…" aloud. Vocabulary on the board: connection · network · switch. Same routine as every lesson.

## What is a connection?
![Two people connected]({{res:l4-two-people-connected-teach-computing.png}})

- a connection is a LINK between two things
- a link lets them share with each other
- you are connected to family, friends and teachers

> 🧑‍🏫 Concrete start: who would you tell good news? Those are your connections. Then move from people to computers.

## What is a network?
![A network of connected dots]({{res:l4-a-network-of-connected-dots-teach-computing.png}})

- a **network** = many things connected together
- computers in a network can share information

> 🧑‍🏫 Split the word: "net" (a web of links) + "work" (helps us get things done). Keep the dots picture calm — point, do not animate.

## A switch in the middle
![A network switch]({{res:l4-a-network-switch-teach-computing.png}})

- joining every computer to every other one needs LOTS of cables
- instead, join them all to one **switch**
- the switch passes each message to the right computer

> 🧑‍🏫 I-do: show one switch with arms out to each computer — far fewer cables. The switch does NOT read your message; it just sends it to the right place.

## Order the message journey
- computer 1 → switch → computer 2
- drag the steps into order on the activity worksheet

> 🧑‍🏫 You-do: the `order` task. Likely error: thinking the message goes through other computers — fix-words "it goes to the switch, then straight to the right computer." TA: prompt, do not order it for them.

## Be the network
- you are the computers
- pass a message to a friend THROUGH the switch
- the switch sends it to the right person

> 🧑‍🏫 Calm role-play. Use string/rope as the connection if helpful. Screenshot or photo for show-your-work. Movement break is routine, not a reward.

## I can…
Tick your four "I can…". Tell me what the switch does.

> 🧑‍🏫 Check pupils can say "the switch passes the message to the right computer." Next lesson: more network devices — server and wireless access point.
