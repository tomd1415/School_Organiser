## What we are learning
Today I will name the parts that join our school network, and say why a network is useful.

## How the parts are joined
Network devices are joined with **network cables** that plug into **network sockets** in the wall.

![A network cable]({{res:l6-a-network-cable-teach-computing.png}})

A **printer** is a networked device too — many computers can send work to one printer.

![A printer]({{res:l6-a-networked-printer-teach-computing.png}})

## Sort — wired or wireless?
Some devices join with a cable. Some join with Wi-Fi (no cable). Drag each one.

```sort
Joins with a cable: desktop computer, server, printer
Joins with Wi-Fi (no cable): tablet, laptop
```
