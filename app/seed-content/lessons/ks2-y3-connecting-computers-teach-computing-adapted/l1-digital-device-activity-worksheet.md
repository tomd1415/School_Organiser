## What we are learning

## Input, process, output
- **Input** = what goes IN (you press a key)
- **Process** = the device does its job
- **Output** = what comes OUT (a letter shows on the screen)

## Put the steps in order

```order

Process: the computer works out what to do
Output: the letter A shows on the screen
```
