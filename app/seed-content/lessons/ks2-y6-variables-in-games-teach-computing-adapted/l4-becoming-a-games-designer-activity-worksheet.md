## What we are learning
Today I will choose the artwork for my game and write an algorithm for it.

## Choose your artwork
Your game will be like Fruit catcher. You need to choose **two sprites** to fall down, and a **background**.

Sort these ideas into sprites and backgrounds.

```sort
Sprites (things on the stage): falling apple, a star, a bug
Background (the scene behind): a park, the sky, a beach
```
