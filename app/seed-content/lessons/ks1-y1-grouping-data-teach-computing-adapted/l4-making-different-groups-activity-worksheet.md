## What we are learning
Today I will group the same objects in two ways and count how many share a property.

## Group by colour
Drag each object under its colour.

```sort

Not green: red car, blue car, black cat
```

## Now group the SAME objects another way
This time, group them by what they are.

```sort
Animals: green frog, tree frog, black cat
Cars: red car, blue car
```
