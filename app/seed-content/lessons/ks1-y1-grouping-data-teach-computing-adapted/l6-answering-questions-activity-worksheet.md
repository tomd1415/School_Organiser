## What we are learning
Today I will group objects to answer a question, then record and share what I found.

## The question
**How many cats are there?**

## Plan it in order
Put the steps in order to answer the question.

```order
Read the question
Choose how to group (cat and not cat)
Sort the objects into the two groups
Count the "cat" group
Write down your answer
```

## Sort to answer
Drag each object into a group.

```sort
Cat: kitten, tabby cat
Not cat: red car, blue car, green frog
```
