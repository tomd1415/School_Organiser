# Execute your own project

## Today we are learning
- finish the planning documents
- create the project products
- develop a testing table
- use feedback to improve the work

> 🧑‍🏫 Read the four "I can…" aloud. Vocabulary on the board: execute · sketch · testing table · feedback. Same routine as every lesson.

## Starter — ready to execute?
- rate your confidence with formulas
- recap the homework formulas

> 🧑‍🏫 Use the confidence rating to spot who needs more support before the build.

## Sketch first
- sketch the poster and business card
- think about house style, audience and purpose

> 🧑‍🏫 Planning the visuals saves time and rework. This is on the plan you made.

## Build the products
- the sales sheet (with formulas)
- the poster and the business card

> 🧑‍🏫 30 minutes, paced in 5-minute countdowns. Likely error: rushing and skipping a formula. Fix-words: "build one part, then check it."

## Test it
- write a testing table for the sales sheet
- write the expected result, then the actual result

> 🧑‍🏫 At least five tests. This feeds the final evaluation next lesson.

## Feedback
- swap with your partner
- give specific, kind feedback and make changes

> 🧑‍🏫 Use the tracking sheet. Screenshot the products and testing table onto the worksheet.

Tick your four "I can…". Show me one product and one test.
