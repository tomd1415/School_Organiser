## What we are learning
Today I will trim my recording to remove mistakes and quiet bits, and learn what a podcast is.

## Trim a mistake
To remove a part of your recording, do these steps in order. Drag them into the right order.

```order
Look at the waveform to find the part to remove
Select that part with the mouse
Listen to the selection to check it is the right bit
Press delete to remove it
Listen again to check it sounds right
```
