## What we are learning
Today I will record my own voice on a computer and think about who owns a recording.

## Sort the devices
Drag each device into the right group.

```sort
Records sound IN (input): microphone, headset
Plays sound OUT (output): speaker, headphones
```

## Record your voice
1. Open Audacity.
2. Click the **record** button (the red circle) and say one sentence.
3. Click **stop**. Click **play** to hear it.
4. If you do not like it, **delete** it and try again.
