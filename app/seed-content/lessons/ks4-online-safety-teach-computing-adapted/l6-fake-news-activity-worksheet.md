## 🟡 Core
Put the steps to check a news story in order.

```order
Read past the headline.
Check who wrote it and when.
Look for the same story on trusted sites.
Reverse image search the photo.
```

## Sort — trusted or not trusted?
```sort
More trusted: A well-known news organisation, A story backed by several sources, An article with a named author
Less trusted: A random social media post, A website copying a real news name, A story with no date or author
```
