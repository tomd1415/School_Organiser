## What is an online reputation?
- it is what people can see about you online
- it is also what they think of you because of it
- it includes things OTHER people post about you

> 🧑‍🏫 Analogy: a reputation in real life is what people think of you. Online is the same, but it can last a long time. Use the starter single choice.

## What adds to it?
- public posts
- photos others tag you in
- usernames, comments, likes you share publicly

> 🧑‍🏫 Common misconception: "only what I post counts." Fix-words: "other people add to your reputation too." Matches the starter multi-select.

## Build a positive reputation
- post helpful, polite content
- check your privacy settings
- think before you post

> 🧑‍🏫 This is the card sort and the order task on the activity worksheet. Keep it positive — the goal is good habits, not fear.

## How a reputation is attacked
- a CYBERBULLY posts lies to upset someone
- a HACKER breaks into an account and pretends to be them
- a SCAMMER tricks an account's contacts

> 🧑‍🏫 Matches the matching task. Remind the class: if anyone is targeted, the answer is always REPORT and BLOCK, and tell a trusted adult. TA: watch for anyone who needs a quiet word.
