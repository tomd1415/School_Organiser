## Spot the scam
Read this message:

> "URGENT! Your account will be CLOSED today. Click http://secure-login-verify.example to confirm your password and bank details now. — The Bnak Team"

| Question | Tick ALL that apply |
|---|---|
| Tick ALL the signs that this message is a scam. | [ ] it rushes you to act NOW [ ] it asks for your password and bank details [ ] it has a strange link and a spelling mistake [ ] it is from your saved contacts list |

## 🟡 Core
What should you do if you get a suspicious message? Put the steps in order.

```order
Stop — do not click anything.
Check the sender and the link carefully.
Do not give any personal information.
Report it and delete it.
```

## Sort — safe or risky?
```sort
Safe action: Using a strong, different password, Turning on two-factor login, Checking a link before clicking
Risky action: Using the same password everywhere, Clicking links in unexpected emails, Sharing a password with a stranger
```
