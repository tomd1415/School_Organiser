## Good email etiquette
- a clear SUBJECT line
- a polite greeting (Dear Mr Williams)
- short and to the point
- a sign-off with your name

> 🧑‍🏫 This matches the card sort. "Netiquette" = manners online. Keep examples concrete.

## Poor email etiquette
- ALL CAPITALS (it looks like shouting)
- lots of emojis
- replying while angry
- a silly email address

> 🧑‍🏫 Likely error: thinking CAPS means "important". Fix-words: "capitals look like shouting — it can upset the reader."

## The order to write an email
1. subject line
2. greeting
3. short, clear message
4. re-read and check the tone
5. add your name and send

> 🧑‍🏫 Matches the activity "order" task. Stress step 4: always re-read before sending, especially if you feel emotional.
