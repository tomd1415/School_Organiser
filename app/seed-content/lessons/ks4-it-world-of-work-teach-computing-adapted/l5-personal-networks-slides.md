## What is an ad hoc network?
![A phone tethered to a laptop by a cable]({{res:l5-tethering.png}})

- AD HOC = a quick, temporary, device-to-device connection
- PAN = Personal Area Network (a small network around you)
- ways to make one: Bluetooth, tethering, a mobile hotspot

> 🧑‍🏫 Keep it concrete with the tethering photo. Each pupil should be able to name one method by the end.

## Good and bad of ad hoc networks
- GOOD: cheap, quick, flexible, useful in an emergency
- BAD: more open to attackers, slower data, no device in control

> 🧑‍🏫 Matches the activity card sort. Balance it: handy but less secure.

## Staying safe — the VPN
- a VPN sends your data through a private server
- it keeps your data hidden from attackers
- use one on open or public WiFi

> 🧑‍🏫 Simple line: "a VPN is a private tunnel for your data." Links to the matching task and the scenarios.
