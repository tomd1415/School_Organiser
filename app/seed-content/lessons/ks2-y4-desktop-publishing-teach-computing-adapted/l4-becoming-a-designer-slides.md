# Becoming a designer

## Today we are learning
- find and open my saved template
- paste text and pictures to make a magazine cover
- make changes to my content after I add it

> 🧑‍🏫 Read the three "I can…" aloud. Vocabulary on the board: content · copy · paste · editorial designer. Same routine.

## The editorial designer
- an EDITORIAL DESIGNER decides where text and pictures go
- they make the magazine look good so people want to read it
- today YOU are the editorial designer

> 🧑‍🏫 Age-respectful framing: this is a real job. Pupils are doing the designer's work today.

## Find your work
- open the app
- find your magazine template from last lesson
- it is saved in your recent designs

> 🧑‍🏫 Show where saved work lives in your chosen app. Reassure anyone who cannot find theirs — TA helps locate it.

## Copy and paste
- COPY the text you want
- click where it should go
- PASTE it into the placeholder

> 🧑‍🏫 This is the starter "order" task. Likely error: pasting into the wrong box. Fix-words: "click the right box first, then paste."

## Add a picture
- search for a picture
- click to add it
- drag it and resize it to fit

> 🧑‍🏫 Remind pupils of the school rule for searching for images. Model adding and resizing one picture. You-do on the activity worksheet.
