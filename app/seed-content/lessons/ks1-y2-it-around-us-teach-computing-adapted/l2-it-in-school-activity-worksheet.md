## What we are learning
Today I will sort school IT by what it is used for, and see that some IT can be used in more than one way.

We use IT in school for different jobs. A computer can help us **write**, **find things out**, and **play and learn**.

![A desktop computer]({{res:l2-desktop-computer.png}})

## Sort the school IT
Drag each piece of IT into the job we use it for.

```sort
Write or make: a keyboard, a printer
Find things out: a tablet, an interactive whiteboard
Play and learn: a computer game, a learning app
```
