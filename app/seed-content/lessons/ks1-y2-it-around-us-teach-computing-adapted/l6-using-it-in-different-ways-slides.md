# Using IT in different ways

## Today we are learning
- see the choices we make when using IT
- use IT for different types of activities
- explain why we use IT in different ways

> 🧑‍🏫 Read the three "I can…" aloud. Last lesson of the unit. Same calm routine.

## We make choices with IT
- we choose WHAT to do
- we choose HOW LONG for
- it is good to try a mix, not just one thing

> 🧑‍🏫 Starter: pupils share a favourite IT activity. Big idea: a "balanced diet" of activities keeps our brains and bodies happy.

## Different types of IT activities
![A paint palette and brushes]({{res:l6-a-paint-palette-and-brushes-teach-computing.png}})

- **connect** — video call, send a message
- **get creative** — paint, write a story
- **be active or rest** — exercise video, screen break

> 🧑‍🏫 These three types are the card-sort on the worksheet. Keep the words simple. Model one example for each.

## Try each type
- connect: send a kind message
- get creative: paint a picture
- be active: do an exercise video
- be mindful: take a calm screen break

> 🧑‍🏫 The stations / you-do. Allow transition time between activities. Each pupil photographs what they made or did for show-your-work. Likely error: wanting to stay on one favourite — fix-words "we are trying a mix today."

## Why use IT in different ways?
- it keeps our brains busy in different ways
- too much of one thing is not good for us
- a mix is more fun and more healthy

> 🧑‍🏫 This matches the Challenge question. Plenary: "How does IT help us? What do you like or dislike about using IT?"

## I can…
Tick your three "I can…". Tell me two different ways to use IT.
