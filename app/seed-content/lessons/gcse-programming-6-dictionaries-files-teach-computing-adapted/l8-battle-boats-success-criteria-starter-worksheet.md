## What we are learning
Today I will learn the rules of **Battle Boats** and write the **success criteria** for the program I will build.

## Starter — play Battle Boats
You will play one round on paper with a partner. Each player has two 8×8 grids: a **fleet grid** (your 5 boats, marked `B`) and a **target tracker** (your shots — `H` for hit, `M` for miss). Take turns firing at a coordinate. The first to sink all 5 enemy boats wins.
