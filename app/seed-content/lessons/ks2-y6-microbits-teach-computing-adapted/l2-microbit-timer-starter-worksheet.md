## Why do we need a timer?
With only a counter, two people could take **different** amounts of time. That is not a fair competition. A **timer** gives everyone the **same** amount of time.

## A loop repeats steps
A **forever** loop repeats the same steps again and again. A countdown uses a loop to take 1 away, wait, and show the number, over and over.
