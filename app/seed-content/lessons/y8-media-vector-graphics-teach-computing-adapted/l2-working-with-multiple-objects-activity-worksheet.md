## What we are learning
Today I will select several objects at once, align and distribute them, group them, and combine shapes.

## Group or combine?
Drag each fact into the right column.

```sort
Group: can be ungrouped again later, the shapes stay separate, used to move objects together
Combine: makes one single new object, cannot be ungrouped, uses union or difference
```
