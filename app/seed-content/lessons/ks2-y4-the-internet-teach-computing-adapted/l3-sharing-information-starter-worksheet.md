## What we are learning
Today I will learn what we can share on the World Wide Web, where websites are stored, and how we get to them.

## Starter — can it be shared on the web?
We can share **media** on the web: pictures, videos, text and sounds.
We cannot share real, physical things like a sandwich or a pet.
