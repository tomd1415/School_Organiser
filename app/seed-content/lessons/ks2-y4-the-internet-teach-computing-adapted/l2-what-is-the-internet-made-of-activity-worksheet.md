## What we are learning
Today I will learn that the internet gives us many services, and that the World Wide Web is part of the internet.

## The internet gives us services
The internet is the wires, routers and devices. It carries many **services**:
- the **World Wide Web** (websites and web pages)
- sharing **files**
- **email**

The World Wide Web is just ONE part of the internet. They are not the same thing.

## Sort — internet or World Wide Web?

```sort
The internet: wires and cables, routers, sending an email, sharing a file

```

## Website and web page
A **website** is a collection of pages under one name.
A **web page** is a single page from that website.
