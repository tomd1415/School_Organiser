# Connecting networks

## Today we are learning
- describe the internet as a network of networks
- show how information is shared across the internet
- name a router and say what it does
- say why a network needs protecting

> 🧑‍🏫 Read the four "I can…" aloud. Vocabulary on the board: internet · network · router · network security. Same routine as every lesson.

## Remember — a network
- a **network** = computers joined together to share
- in a network, a **switch** passes the message to the right computer

> 🧑‍🏫 Recap from Year 3. Quick check: "what does the switch do?" Answer wanted: passes the message to the right computer. Keep it concrete.

## The internet = a network of networks
![Internet cables joining the whole world]({{res:l1-submarine-cable-map.png}})

- the **internet** joins lots of networks together
- it reaches all around the world through cables

> 🧑‍🏫 The lines are real cables, many under the sea, joining countries. Point, do not animate. The big idea: the internet is networks joined to networks.

## A router joins networks
![A router]({{res:l1-router.png}})

- a **switch** passes a message inside ONE network
- a **router** passes a message to ANOTHER network

> 🧑‍🏫 I-do: "Switch stays Same network. Router Reaches another network." Likely error: mixing up switch and router — fix-words "the Router Reaches Another network."

## Order the message journey
- computer → switch → router → switch → computer
- drag the steps into order on the activity worksheet

> 🧑‍🏫 You-do: the `order` task crosses TWO networks. Likely error: thinking the message skips the router — fix-words "to reach another network, it must go through the router." TA: prompt, do not order it for them.

## Keep the network safe
![A safe shield and an unsafe shield]({{res:l1-security-shields.png}})

- **network security** keeps a network safe
- it lets the right people in and keeps others out

> 🧑‍🏫 Keep this cohort-level and calm: a network is protected so only the right people use it, like a locked door for the school. No personal online-safety stories.

## Be the internet
- you are computers on different networks
- pass a message through the switch and the router
- the router takes it to the other network

> 🧑‍🏫 Calm role-play. Change who is the switch/router from last year's lesson. Screenshot or photo for show-your-work. Movement break is routine, not a reward.
