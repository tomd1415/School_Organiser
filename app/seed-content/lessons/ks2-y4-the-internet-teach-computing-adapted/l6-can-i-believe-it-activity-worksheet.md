## What we are learning
Today I will say why some information online is not honest or accurate, and why I should think before I share.

## Why is some information not true?
Some people put information online that is not true. There are different reasons why.

![Money — some false information is shared to make money]({{res:l6-money.png}})

## Why might someone share false information?
| Reason | What it means |
|---|---|
| To make money | False claims to get you to buy something. |
| To be popular | Saying something untrue to get more likes. |
| By mistake | Sharing it without knowing it is untrue. |

## Order — how information spreads
Once you share something, it can spread fast and you cannot take it back. Drag the steps into order.

```order
One person shares a piece of information
Their friends see it and share it too
Many more people share it
It has spread, and you cannot take it back
```
