## What we are learning
Today I will say what media is on websites, and use a web tool to create my own content.

## Adding content to the web
Websites are made by **people**. On some websites, anyone can **add content** — like a blog, a comment, or a project they made.

| Good thing about anyone adding content | Tricky thing about anyone adding content |
|---|---|
| It is easy to share what you know. | Not everything added is true or kind. |

## Make your own content
A web tool lets you create content online. Today you will use **Chrome Music Lab** to make a sound or pattern.
