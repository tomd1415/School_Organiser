## What we are learning
Today I will explain the Freedom of Information Act, name the three levels of the Computer Misuse Act, and decide which level a case study breaks.

## The three levels of the Computer Misuse Act
- **Level 1** — getting into a computer you are not allowed to use
- **Level 2** — getting in, planning to commit a further crime
- **Level 3** — getting in and damaging or changing things

Drag each real case under the level it breaks.

```sort
CMA level 1: An ex-employee keeps accessing the company email after leaving
CMA level 2: Someone installs a keylogger to steal passwords and redirect funds
CMA level 2: An employee hacks their firm to defraud it but is caught first
CMA level 3: A nurse uses a stolen password to change patient records
CMA level 3: A gamer hacks 4000 accounts and changes 100 of them
Not a breach: Someone makes fake social media accounts but never hacks a computer
```
