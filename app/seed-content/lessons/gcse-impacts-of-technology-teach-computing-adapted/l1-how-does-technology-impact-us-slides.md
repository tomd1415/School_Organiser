## The law — who keeps your data safe?
- **data subject** — the person the data is about (you)
- **data controller** — the organisation that holds the data
- **ICO** — the UK regulator that checks the rules are followed

> 🧑‍🏫 These three terms come up all unit. Slow and clear. The Data Protection Act 2018 is the UK law.

## The Data Protection Act — six promises
- data must be used fairly, kept accurate, kept safe
- only collect what you need, and not for longer than you need

> 🧑‍🏫 Worked example (I do): a shop only needs your address to deliver, not your date of birth. We do: pick one principle for Sophia's farm together. You do: pupils finish on the worksheet.
