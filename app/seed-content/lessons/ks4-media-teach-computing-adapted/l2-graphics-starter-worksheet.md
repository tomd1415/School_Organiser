## What we are learning
Today I will learn the two types of digital graphic — raster and vector — and how to make them.

## Starter — two types of graphic
- a **raster** graphic is made of tiny coloured squares called **pixels** (like a photo). It goes blurry if you make it much bigger.
- a **vector** graphic is made of **maths paths**. It can be made any size and stays sharp.
