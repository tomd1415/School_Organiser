# Planning your digital media artefact

## Today we are learning
- read a client brief and choose one
- choose a media artefact to make
- plan the artefact with your group
- use the rubric to aim higher

> 🧑‍🏫 Read the four "I can…" aloud. Vocabulary on the board: digital media artefact · client brief · stock materials · delegation · collaboration · rubric. Groups of 4–5 for the rest of the unit.

## Starter — key words
- digital media artefact = the media product you make (logo, poster, video, website)
- client brief = the document saying what the client wants
- delegation = sharing the jobs out

## Choose your brief
- four briefs: game app · farming robots · medical AI · driverless truck
- every brief needs a logo, then pick ONE artefact: poster, video, or website

> 🧑‍🏫 Give 10 minutes to choose. Tell them to write WHY they chose it — that reasoning is part of the final presentation.

## Plan as a team
- pick your pre-production tools (mood board, storyboard, script)
- delegate the jobs
- write down the plan and the reasons

> 🧑‍🏫 The activity worksheet has these steps to order, then a job list. TA: prompt quieter members to take a real job. No flashing/sound; movement breaks are routine.
