## Starter — highlight by hand
Colour every number over 20 green on your sheet.

> 🧑‍🏫 Then explain: the spreadsheet can do this automatically — that is conditional formatting.

## Conditional formatting
![Conditional formatting]({{res:l6-conditional-formatting.png}})

Set a rule (for example "greater than 10,000") and the spreadsheet colours the matching cells.

> 🧑‍🏫 Use the single-colour option and a predefined rule. Model one rule on the demo data.
