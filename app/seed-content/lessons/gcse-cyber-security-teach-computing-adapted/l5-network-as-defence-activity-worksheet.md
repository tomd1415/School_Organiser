## Allow or block?
The school rule: students can move files smaller than 5GB; gaming and illegal file-sharing sites are blocked. Drag each request into the right box.

```sort
Firewall lets it through (follows the rules): a student downloads a 1GB revision file, a teacher uploads a 10GB training video
Firewall blocks it (breaks the rules): a student uploads an 8GB file, a request to a known online gaming site
```

## How a firewall checks a packet
Drag the steps into the right order, from first to last.

```order
A packet of data arrives at the firewall.
The firewall checks the packet against the network rules.
If the packet breaks a rule, the firewall blocks it.
If the packet follows the rules, the firewall lets it through.
```
