## How a phishing attack works
Drag the steps into the right order, from first to last.

```order
The criminal sends out thousands of fake emails that look real.
The email asks you to click a link or 'confirm' your details.
The link opens a fake website that copies a real one.
You type your password, and the criminal now has it.
```

## Sort the social engineering tricks
Some tricks need the criminal to be there in person. Others can be done from far away. Drag each one into the right box.

```sort
Done in person: shoulder surfing, tailgating, eavesdropping
Done from far away: phishing, pharming, blagging
```
