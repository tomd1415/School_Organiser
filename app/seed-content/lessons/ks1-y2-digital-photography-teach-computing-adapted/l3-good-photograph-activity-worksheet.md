## What we are learning

## Three things to check
A photographer **composes** a photo. Three things help.

- **Positioning** — is it clear what the photo is of?
- **Framing** — is the whole subject in the picture?
- **Detail** — are you close enough to see it clearly?

![A clear, well-composed beach photo]({{res:l3-beach-good.png}})

Put the steps in the right order. Drag them into order.

```order
Choose your subject
Frame it so the whole subject is in the picture
Get close enough to see the detail
Hold still and take the photo
Look at it — could it be better?
```
