## What we are learning
Today I will find out that a photo can be changed on a computer.

## The same photo, changed
**Editing** means changing a photo. Changing the colour gives a different feel.

![A dog photo with normal colours]({{res:l5-a-dog-photo-with-normal-colours-teach-computing.jpg}})
![The same dog with a blue tint]({{res:l5-the-same-dog-with-a-blue-tint-teach-computing.jpg}})
![The same dog with a red tint]({{res:l5-the-same-dog-with-a-red-tint-teach-computing.jpg}})

It is the **same dog** in every photo — only the colour has changed.
