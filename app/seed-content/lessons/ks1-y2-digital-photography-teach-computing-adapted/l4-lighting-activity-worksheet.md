## A different light source
A **torch** or the camera **flash** adds light when a place is too dark.

Put the steps in the right order. Drag them into order.

```order
Take a photo in a dark place
See that the photo is too dark
Add light with a torch or the flash
Take the photo again
Check that the photo is clearer now
```
