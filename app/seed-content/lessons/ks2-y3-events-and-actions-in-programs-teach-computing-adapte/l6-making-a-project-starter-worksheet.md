## What we are learning
We design and build our own maze project. First we **design** it, then we **code** it, then we **test** it.

## Look at the maze
Your sprite will start at **Start** and move to **Finish**.

![A blank maze with a Start corner and a Finish corner]({{res:l6-blank-maze-design-teach-computing.png}})

## Put the steps in order
Drag the five build steps into a sensible order.

```order
Add, resize and recentre your sprite
Choose your event blocks (the keys)
Add the motion blocks (point and move)
Add the pen blocks (draw and set up)
Test your project and debug it
```
