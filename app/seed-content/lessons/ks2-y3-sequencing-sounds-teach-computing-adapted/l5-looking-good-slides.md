# Looking good

## Today we are learning
- build a sequence with motion and sound
- change a sprite using costumes
- change the stage using backdrops
- make design choices for my project

> 🧑‍🏫 Read the four "I can…" aloud. Vocabulary on the board: sprite · stage · costume · backdrop · sequence. Same routine as every lesson.

## Motion and sound together
![The motion blocks you can use]({{res:l5-motion-blocks.png}})

- a sequence can mix a move block and a sound block
- predict: move then sound = the sprite moves, then plays
- run it to check

> 🧑‍🏫 Starter (10 min). Show a short sequence with a move and a sound. Pupils predict on the worksheet, then run it. Praise good predictions.

## Costumes and backdrops
- a costume changes how the SPRITE looks
- a backdrop changes the STAGE behind it
- both make your project look better

> 🧑‍🏫 I-do → we-do. Likely error: mixing up costume and backdrop. Fix-words: "costume = the sprite's clothes; backdrop = the room behind it."

## Design your project
- plan three sprites on the worksheet
- each: a sound + a movement + a look change
- choose a backdrop
- then build it in Scratch

> 🧑‍🏫 You-do (about 26 min). Design first, then build. Offer the part-finished plan to anyone who needs more scaffold. TA: prompt, do not do it for them. Screenshot the result.

## Share
- show a partner
- say one thing you designed

> 🧑‍🏫 Plenary. Quick share. Reassure that finishing happens next lesson too.
