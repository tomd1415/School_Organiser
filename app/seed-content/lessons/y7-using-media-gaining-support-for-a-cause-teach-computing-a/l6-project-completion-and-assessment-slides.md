# Project completion and assessment

## Today we are learning
- finish and share the blog
- check the blog against the assessment criteria
- answer questions about using media
- reflect on what I have learned

> 🧑‍🏫 Read the four "I can…" aloud. Vocabulary: blog · assessment criteria · feedback · summative.

## Starter — share your blog
Publish or share your blog so the class and teacher can see it.

> 🧑‍🏫 Show how to make a share link / publish. Then 5 minutes for pupils to view each other's blogs for ideas. Use the starter worksheet to record the share link.

## Check against the criteria  (we do)
Audience · sources · formatting · images.

![The assessment rubric: audience, sources, formatting, images]({{res:l6-assessment-rubric-teach-computing.png}})

> 🧑‍🏫 Show the rubric on the board and recap each row, so pupils know what to finish.

## Finish your blog  (you do)
Use your peer feedback to make your last improvements.

> 🧑‍🏫 20 minutes to finish. If a common software question comes up, gather a small group at the board. TA: prompt, do not do it for them.

## End-of-unit quiz
Answer the questions on the assessment worksheet. Pick the one best answer.

> 🧑‍🏫 10–15 minutes, single best answer each time. Calm, low-pressure. The questions cover software, formatting, copyright and credibility.

## I didn't know how to… but now I can
Write one thing you can do now that you could not do before.

> 🧑‍🏫 Two minutes to reflect; pupils add a note to the board. Read a few out to celebrate progress.
