# Research and plan your blog

## Today we are learning
- explain plagiarism, citation and paraphrase
- reference a source correctly
- judge whether a source is reliable
- record research and credit sources

> 🧑‍🏫 Read the four "I can…" aloud. Vocabulary: plagiarism · referencing · citation · paraphrase · blog.

## Starter — three key words  (I do)
Citation · paraphrase · plagiarism.

> 🧑‍🏫 Use the starter worksheet (match the word to its meaning). Hint pupils to type "define" before a word when searching. Likely error: mixing up paraphrase and plagiarism → fix-words: "paraphrase still needs a credit."

## Is this plagiarism?  (we do)
A poem with a few words changed, shared as your own.

> 🧑‍🏫 Think · pair · share. Answer: it IS plagiarism — too similar and no credit to the author. Changing words is not enough.
