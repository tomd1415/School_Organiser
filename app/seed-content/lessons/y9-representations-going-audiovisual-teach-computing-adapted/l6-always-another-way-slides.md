## Another language
- a **vector** image stores shapes and instructions, not pixels
- **MIDI** music stores notes and instructions, not samples

## Compression — why we need it
Media files can be huge. Compression makes them smaller to store and send.

![A kingfisher photo — the file can be compressed]({{res:l6-kingfisher-photo-teach-computing.jpg}})

> 🧑‍🏫 Worked example on the worksheet: 2.2 MB photo → ~106 kB compressed, about 20× smaller. Same image, far fewer bits.
