## Look — a picture made of small squares
This street art is a **mosaic**: a picture built from small coloured squares. Digital pictures work the same way — the small squares are called **pixels**.

```label
image: {{res:l1-binary-mosaic-street-art-teach-computing.jpg}}
z1 (40%, 42%): one pixel
z2 (52%, 35%): a row
z3 (33%, 60%): a column
```
