## How nested selection flows
Drag the steps into the right order.

```order
ask "does it live in water?"
if the answer is not n, say "it must be a Whale"
if the answer is n, ask "does it have wings?"
if it has wings, say "it must be an Ostrich"
if it does not have wings, say "it must be a Lion"
```
