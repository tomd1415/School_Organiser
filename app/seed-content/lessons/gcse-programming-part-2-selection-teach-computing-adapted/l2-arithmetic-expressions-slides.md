## BIDMAS — the order of operations
Brackets · Indices · Division/Multiplication · Addition/Subtraction.

> 🧑‍🏫 Same rules as maths. Multiply/divide before add/subtract; brackets first of all.

## Python operators
`+`  `-`  `*`  `/`  `//`  `%`  `**`

> 🧑‍🏫 Most are familiar. New ones: `//` whole-number divide, `%` remainder (modulo), `**` power. Pupils match these on the worksheet.

## Integer division // and modulo %
- `7 // 3` → 2  (how many whole 3s fit)
- `7 % 3` → 1  (what is left over)

> 🧑‍🏫 Misconception: thinking `10 % 3` is 0.333. Modulo is the WHOLE remainder. Use counters: 10 counters into groups of 3 → 3 groups, 1 left over.
