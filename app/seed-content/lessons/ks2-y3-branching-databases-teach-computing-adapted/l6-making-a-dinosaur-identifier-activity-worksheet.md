## Real-world uses
Branching databases are used for real jobs. Sort each job into "a branching database could do this" or not.

```sort
A branching database could do this: name a plant from its leaves, find the fault in a broken machine, work out which animal you saw
Not a job for a branching database: paint a picture, sing a song, kick a ball
```
