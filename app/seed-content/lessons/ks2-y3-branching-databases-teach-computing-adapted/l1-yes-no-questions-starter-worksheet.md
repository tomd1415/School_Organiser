## What we are learning
Today I will tell a yes/no question from an open question, and make up my own yes/no question.

## Two kinds of question
Some questions only need a **yes** or a **no**. Other questions are open — they need a longer answer.

Drag each question into the right box.

```sort
Yes/no question: Is it red?, Does it have wheels?, Can it fly?
Open question: What is your favourite colour?, Why do you like it?, How does it work?
```
