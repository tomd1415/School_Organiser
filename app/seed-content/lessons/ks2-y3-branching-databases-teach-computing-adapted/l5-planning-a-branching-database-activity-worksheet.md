## What we are learning
Today I will plan a dinosaur branching database on paper, with my own yes/no questions.

![Dinosaur cards]({{res:l5-dinosaur-cards.png}})

## Plan the steps
Put the planning steps in the right order.

```order
Look at the dinosaurs and their attributes
Write yes/no questions about the attributes
Choose a first question that splits them in half
Arrange the cards and questions into a tree
Keep splitting until each dinosaur is on its own
Test the plan with a partner
```
