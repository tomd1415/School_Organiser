## Question-type strategy
Every lesson: a short **starter** worksheet (shared single-choice + S/C/C: Support recognition / Core recall / Challenge multi-select) and an **activity** worksheet carrying the main drag task + a required `## Show your work` (📷) + `## ✅ I can…`. Slides: `# title`, one `## ` per slide, `> 🧑‍🏫` teacher notes (likely-error fix-words + TA cues throughout).

Types used: **label** (L2 computer parts, L5 keyboard keys — the two strongest visual fits), **card-sort** (L1 technology/not, L6 safe/not-safe), **order** (L3 open-a-program, L4 save-steps, L5 open-a-file), **matching** (L1 tech→job, L2 part→job, L6 rule→why; rendered as repeated single-choice rows), **multi-select** (challenge tiers), **single-choice**, **text**, **screenshot**.
