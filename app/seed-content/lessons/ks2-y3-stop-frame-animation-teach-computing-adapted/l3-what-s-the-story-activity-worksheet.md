## What we are learning
Today I will make a storyboard and choose a story that is simple to animate.

## Order the storyboard
A storyboard shows the start, the middle and the end. Drag the parts into order.

```order
Start: the two characters stand still
Middle: one character walks forward
End: the character waves
```
