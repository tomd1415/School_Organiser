# Lights, camera, action

## Today we are learning
- name media I can add to a film
- add media to my animation
- say why my media makes the film better

> 🧑‍🏫 Read the three "I can…" aloud. Vocabulary on the board: media · import · title · music · transition. Recap: last lesson we made our animation the best it could be.

## What is media?
- music
- a title
- a picture
- a sound

> 🧑‍🏫 Media means the extra things we add to a film. This is the sort task on the starter worksheet. Keep examples simple and concrete.

## The movie app

- Video adds a film
- Photos adds a picture

> 🧑‍🏫 Import the saved animation, then show the tabs. This is the label task on the activity worksheet. Likely error: looking for music in the wrong tab → fix-words "music is in the Audio tab."

## Add a title and music
- add a title so people see the name
- add music that fits the film
- keep the volume low

> 🧑‍🏫 I-do → we-do. Add one title and one piece of music. Any source example films are teacher-played only — keep them low arousal.

## Your turn — add your media
- add a title
- add some music
- watch it back

> 🧑‍🏫 You-do: pupils add media to their film. TA: prompt, do not do it for them. Support: find the Audio tab. Challenge: say why the music fits.
