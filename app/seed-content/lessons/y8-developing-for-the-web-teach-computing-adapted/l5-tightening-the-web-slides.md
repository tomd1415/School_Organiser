## Operators control results  (I do)
- `OR` → either word → MORE results (broadens)
- `NOT` (`-`) → removes a word → FEWER results (narrows)
- `" "` quotes → the exact phrase → FEWER results (narrows)

> 🧑‍🏫 Use the animal groups example: "lives in water AND on land" narrows; "milk OR many legs" broadens. AND/NOT cut down, OR opens up.

## Try the searches  (we do)
Find two scientists with only `Turing OR Babbage`. Find a USA Taj Mahal restaurant, `-"new york"`.

> 🧑‍🏫 Results vary by search engine and over time — that is fine. The skill is choosing the operator on purpose.
