## Starter — working hyperlink or not?
`<a href="home.html">Home</a>` works. `<a href="home.html">Home<a>` does not.

> 🧑‍🏫 Draw out: the link must close with `</a>`, and it is `href`, not `link`. Spot the errors on the starter worksheet.
