## What we are learning
Today I will recommend a storage device for a situation, and describe what cloud storage is.

## How to choose a device
To choose a device, read the situation and look for clues: how much data (capacity), does it need to travel (portable), does it need to be tough (durable), how much can they spend (cost)?
