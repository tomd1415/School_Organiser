# Creating a brand

## Today we are learning
- plan a consistent layout for three slides
- recolour a logo to match the style
- make a styled set of slides from a plan

> 🧑‍🏫 Read the three "I can…" aloud. Vocabulary on board: brand · logo. This lesson is about STYLE, not content — content comes next lesson.

## Starter — branding or content?
Some parts of a slide are branding (stay the same). Some are content (change).

> 🧑‍🏫 Pupils sort on the starter worksheet. Branding = logo, colours, shapes. Content = titles, text, images.

## The task — three branded slides  (I do)
A charity wants three blank branded slides with a unified look and a logo.

> 🧑‍🏫 Read the three charity missions: Our Identity · No to Bullies! · Age Appropriate. Pupils pick one.

## Plan your style  (we do)
Choose your logo, your colours, and your font. Keep them the same on every slide.

![Safety logo]({{res:l4-safety-logo-teach-computing.png}}) ![Stranger-danger logo]({{res:l4-stranger-danger-logo-teach-computing.png}}) ![Warning logo]({{res:l4-warning-logo-teach-computing.png}})

> 🧑‍🏫 Use the colour tool again. Black or white text is often best. Pupils fill the plan on the activity worksheet and check it against the rubric.

## Recolour your logo  (you do)
Open your chosen logo. Recolour it to match your plan. Save it with a new name.

> 🧑‍🏫 Same fill-tool skill as Lesson 3. Likely error: colours don't match the slides → fix-words "use your plan's main colour".

## Make your three slides  (you do)
Open the three blank slides. Style them to your plan. Add your logo. Add example text so you can set the font.

> 🧑‍🏫 Support: keep the logo in the same spot each slide. Challenge: consistent shades across all three. Pupils screenshot all three on the worksheet.

## I can…
Tick your three "I can…". Show me your three matching slides. Check yourself against the rubric.

> 🧑‍🏫 Note who kept a consistent look across all three slides.
