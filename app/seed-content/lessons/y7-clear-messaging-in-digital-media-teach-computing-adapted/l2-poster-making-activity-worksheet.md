## What we are learning
Today I will plan a poster, choose and download a suitable image, and start making my poster.

## Vocabulary
- **Portrait** — taller than it is wide. **Landscape** — wider than it is tall.
- **Download** — save a file from the web onto your computer.
- **Heading** — the big title. **Body text** — the smaller words.
