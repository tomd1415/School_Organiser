## What we are learning
Today I will learn about compasses and comparison operators (< > =).

## What is a compass?
A **compass** shows which direction is north. The micro:bit has a compass inside it. It can tell us a **heading** — a direction shown as a number of degrees.

## Comparison operators
A **comparison operator** compares two numbers. We use `<` (less than), `>` (greater than) and `=` (equal to).
