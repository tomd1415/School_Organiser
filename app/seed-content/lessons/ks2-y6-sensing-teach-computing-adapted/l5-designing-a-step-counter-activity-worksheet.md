## What we are learning
Today I will design my step counter: the variable, the algorithm, and the program flow.

## What our step counter will do
- count steps with the accelerometer (a shake = a step)
- show the number of steps when button B is pressed
- show "Keep it up!" or "Great!" depending on how many steps

![A step counter design: algorithm and program flow]({{res:l5-stepcounter-design.png}})

An **algorithm** is the plan for the program, written as clear steps in order. Put the algorithm steps in order.

```order
Set steps to 0
When the micro:bit is shaken, change steps by 1
When button B is pressed, show the number of steps
If steps is less than 30, show "Keep it up!"
If steps is 30 or more, show "Great!"
```
