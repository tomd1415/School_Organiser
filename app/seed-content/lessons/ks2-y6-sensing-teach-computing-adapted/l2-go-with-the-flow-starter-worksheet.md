## What we are learning
Today I will spot "if… then… else…" choices in real life.

## Making choices
We make choices all day. Many choices have a **condition** — something we check first.

Example:
- **If** it is raining **then** take a coat **else** wear a cap.

The condition is "it is raining". If it is true, we take a coat. If it is false, we wear a cap.
