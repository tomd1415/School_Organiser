## What we are learning
Today I will get to know the micro:bit and its main parts.

## What is a micro:bit?
A micro:bit is a tiny computer. You write programs for it in **MakeCode**. It can take **inputs** (like a button press or a shake) and make **outputs** (like lights or sound).
