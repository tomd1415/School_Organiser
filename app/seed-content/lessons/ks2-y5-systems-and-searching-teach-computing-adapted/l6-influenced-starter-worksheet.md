## What we are learning
Today I will learn how search results can be changed, and how search engines make money.

## Starter — three people in a search
Three people change what you see in a search:
- the SEARCHER — the words you type
- the SEARCH ENGINE — its rules and its adverts
- the PAGE MAKER — the words they put on the page

Drag each action to the right person.

```sort
The searcher: types the words, picks which result to click
The search engine: sets the ranking rules, sells advert spots
The page maker: chooses the words on the page, adds links to the page
```
