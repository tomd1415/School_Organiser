## Make a search better
Some words mean more than one thing. Look at these three:

| ![Rugby town]({{res:l3-rugby.png}}) | ![Sale town]({{res:l3-sale.png}}) | ![Boot village]({{res:l3-boot.png}}) |
|---|---|---|
| Rugby is a town AND a sport | Sale is a town AND money off in shops | Boot is a village AND something you wear |

Sort what each plain search might bring back.

```sort
A town: Rugby in Warwickshire, Sale near Manchester, Boot in Cumbria
The other meaning: a game of rugby, money off in a shop, a boot you wear
```
