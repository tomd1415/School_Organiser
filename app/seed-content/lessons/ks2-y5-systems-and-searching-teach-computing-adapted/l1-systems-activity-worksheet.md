## What we are learning
Today I will name the input, process and output of a digital system, and design a system of my own.

## A digital system
A washing machine is a digital system.
- INPUT = what you put in (you press a button or turn the dial)
- PROCESS = the job it does (it washes the clothes)
- OUTPUT = what comes out (clean clothes and the display)

![A washing machine]({{res:l1-washing-machine.png}})

## Sort the parts
Drag each part into input, process or output.

```sort
Input: pressing a button, turning the dial
Process: the drum spins and washes
Output: the clean clothes, the time on the display
```
