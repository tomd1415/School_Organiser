## A shop system
A catalogue shop (like Argos) is a big system. Some jobs are done by a person, and some by a computer.

![A delivery van]({{res:l2-delivery-van.png}})

Drag each job into person or computer.

```sort
A person does it: hands you your parcel, packs the box, helps you choose
A computer does it: checks if the item is in stock, takes the card payment, prints the receipt
```

Now put the steps of an order in the right order.

```order
You choose the item on the screen
The computer checks it is in stock
You pay at the till
A person brings your parcel
```
