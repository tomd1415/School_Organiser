## What we are learning
Today I will learn that a search engine follows rules to rank pages, and name things that make a page rank higher.

## A search engine follows rules
A search engine follows RULES (an algorithm). It gives each page a score. The page with the best score goes to the TOP.

![A search bar with 'your school' typed in]({{res:l5-google-search.png}})

## Make a webpage and score it
Make a quick paper webpage about your school.

![A school]({{res:l5-school.png}})

Here are the scoring rules for the search word "school":
- the word in the heading = 10 points
- the word in the web address (URL) = 20 points
- another page links to your page = 50 points
