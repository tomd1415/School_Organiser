## What we are learning
Today I will learn the difference between 2D and 3D shapes, and that I can make 3D shapes on a computer.

## 2D and 3D shapes
A **2D** shape is flat. It has width and height, but no depth.
A **3D** shape is solid. It has width, height, and **depth**. You can turn it and look at it from many sides.

## Sort the shapes
Drag each shape into the 2D group or the 3D group.

```sort
2D (flat): square, circle, triangle
3D (solid): cube, sphere, cylinder
```
