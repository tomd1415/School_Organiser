## 2D and 3D
- 2D shapes are flat: a square, a circle
- 3D shapes are solid and have depth: a cube, a sphere
- on the starter worksheet, pupils sort shapes into 2D and 3D

> ЁЯзСтАНЁЯПл Likely error: "a cube is a square." Fix-words: "a square is FLAT; a cube is SOLID тАФ it has depth." Support pupils choose what 3D means; Challenge: why model in 3D on a computer?

## Looking from different sides
![The same shapes from the top]({{res:l1-perspective-top.png}})

- move your view by dragging on the workplane
- the shapes stay still тАФ only your view moves
- this is called perspective

> ЁЯзСтАНЁЯПл The view cube can be sensitive тАФ model it slowly. Support pupils choose what "perspective" means; Core explain that the shapes did not actually move.

## Moving shapes
- click a shape to select it (it turns blue and shows handles)
- drag it to move it, or use the arrow keys
- do not click the handles yet тАФ that resizes the shape

> ЁЯзСтАНЁЯПл Pupils order the "move a shape" steps, then place a cube and sphere next to each other and screenshot it. TA: prompt, do not do it for them.
