## Starter — light it up
In science you learned that a bulb only lights when the **circuit is closed** so current can flow. Put the steps to light an LED in order.

```order
Connect a wire to the power pin (3V)
Connect another wire to the LED
Connect the LED back to the ground pin (GND)
The circuit is now closed
Current flows and the LED lights up
```

## The pins
The gold strips along the bottom are the **GPIO pins**. We can connect wires, switches, speakers and LEDs to them.
