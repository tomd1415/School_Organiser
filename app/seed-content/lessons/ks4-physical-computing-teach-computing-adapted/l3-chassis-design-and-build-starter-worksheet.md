## What we are learning
Today I will plan and build a chassis (the body) for my buggy that is strong enough to hold all the parts.

## Starter — what makes a good chassis?
The chassis has to hold the motors, the board, the battery and the sensors, AND stay together when the buggy moves. Drag each statement into the right group.

```sort
A strong chassis: holds the weight of all the parts, stays together when it moves, keeps the wheels level, has a smooth third support at one end
A weak chassis: bends under the battery weight, falls apart when it moves, lets the wheels point different ways
```
