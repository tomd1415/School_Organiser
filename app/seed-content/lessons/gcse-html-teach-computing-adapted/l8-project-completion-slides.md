# Project completion

## Today we are learning
- act on feedback to finish my website
- show my work to others
- answer end-of-unit questions about HTML and CSS

> 🧑‍🏫 Read the three "I can…" aloud. Keep the assessment criteria on display all lesson.

## Starter — review your feedback
Turn last lesson's peer feedback into a short to-do list on the starter worksheet.

> 🧑‍🏫 Calm start. Help anyone whose feedback is vague to choose one concrete first job.

## Finish the website
Use the rest of the time to work through your to-do list and save your work.

> 🧑‍🏫 Circulate and focus on learners you have flagged as needing help. Make sure work is saved where you can collect it.

## Showcase
Share your website — with the class, or just with the person next to you.

> 🧑‍🏫 Keep it low-pressure: a quiet "show your neighbour" is fine. Praise specific good choices.

## End-of-unit quiz
20 multiple-choice questions on the quiz worksheet. Pick ONE answer each. Take your time.

> 🧑‍🏫 Quiet, individual work. About 15 minutes. Answer key is in the conversion notes. Read questions aloud for anyone who needs it.

## I can…
Tick your three "I can…". Hand in your website and your quiz.

> 🧑‍🏫 Final check: names on quizzes, websites collected. Congratulate the class on finishing the unit.
