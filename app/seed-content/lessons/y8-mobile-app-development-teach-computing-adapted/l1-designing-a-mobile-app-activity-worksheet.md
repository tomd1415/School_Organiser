## User-friendly or not?
A **user-friendly** app is easy and pleasant to use. Drag each one into the right group.

```sort
User-friendly: Big clear buttons, A simple menu, Works on any phone
Not user-friendly: Tiny text you cannot read, Lots of pop-up adverts, Crashes a lot
```

